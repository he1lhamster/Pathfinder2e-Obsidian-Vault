# Deceiver's Cloak<span class="item-type">Focus 3</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Hex</span><span class="item-trait">Illusion</span><span class="item-trait">Witch</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Lesson** [[DATABASE/witchlesson/Lesson of Mischief|lesson of mischief]]
**Cast** <span class="action-icon">2</span> somatic, verbal
**Duration** sustained

---
You wrap yourself in a cloak of illusion, appearing as another creature of the same body shape with roughly similar height and weight as yourself. This has the effects of a 3rd-level [[DATABASE/spell/Illusory Disguise|illusory disguise]].

---
**Heightened (6th)** You can appear as any creature of the same size, even with a completely different body shape.
