**Patron Theme** [[DATABASE/witchpatron/Curse|curse]]
**Cast** <span class="action-icon">1</span> somatic
**Range** 30 feet; **Targets** 1 creature
**Saving Throw** Will; **Duration** sustained up to 1 minute

---
Your fix your eye on the target, imposing a malevolent hex. The target becomes [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened]] based on the results of its Will save. This condition value can't be reduced below 1 while the spell is active and you can see the target. Regardless of the outcome, the target is then temporarily immune for 1 minute.
**Success** The target is unaffected.
**Failure** The target is frightened 1.
**Critical Failure** The target is frightened 2.
