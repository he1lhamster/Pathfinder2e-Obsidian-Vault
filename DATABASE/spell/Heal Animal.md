# Heal Animal<span class="item-type">Focus 1</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Druid</span><span class="item-trait">Healing</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span><span class="item-trait">Positive</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Cast** <span class="action-icon">1</span> to <span class="action-icon">2</span> somatic
**Range** touch or 30 feet (see text); **Targets** 1 willing living animal creature

---
You heal an animal's wounds, restoring 1d8 Hit Points to the target. The number of actions spent Casting this Spell determines its effect.

* <span class="action-icon">1</span> **somatic** The spell has a range of touch. 
* <span class="action-icon">2</span> **somatic, verbal** The spell has a range of 30 feet and restores an additional 8 Hit Points to the target.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The amount of healing increases by 1d8, and the additional healing for the 2-action version increases by 8.
