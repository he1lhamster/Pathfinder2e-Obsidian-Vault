# Imp Sting<span class="item-type">Spell 2</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Evil</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span><span class="item-trait">Poison</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 160. Assault on Hunting Lodge Seven|Pathfinder #160: Assault on Hunting Lodge Seven]]
**Traditions** divine, occult
**Cast** <span class="action-icon">2</span> somatic, verbal
**Range** touch; **Targets** 1 creature
**Saving Throw** Fortitude

---
You reproduce an [[DATABASE/monster/Imp|imp's]] evil, poisonous sting, stabbing at an enemy and possibly affecting them with the devil's benumbing venom. You deal 1d4 piercing damage to the touched creature and afflict the creature with imp venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they also take 1d6 evil damage and must attempt a Fortitude save.
**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.
**Success** The target takes 1d6 poison damage.
**Failure** The target is afflicted with imp venom at stage 1.
**Critical Failure** The target is afflicted with imp venom at stage 2. 
**Imp Venom** (poison) **Level** 3; **Maximum Duration** 6 rounds; **Stage 1** 1d6 poison and [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy 1]]; **Stage 2** 1d6 poison, clumsy 1, and [[DATABASE/condition/Slowed|slowed 1]] (1 round).
