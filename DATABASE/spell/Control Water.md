**Deities** [[DATABASE/deity/Gozreh|Gozreh]], [[DATABASE/deity/Isis|Isis]], [[DATABASE/deity/Lady Nanbyo|Lady Nanbyo]], [[DATABASE/deity/Lysianassa|Lysianassa]], [[DATABASE/deity/Seafarer's Hope|Seafarer's Hope]]
