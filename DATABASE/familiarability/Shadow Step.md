# Shadow Step

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Secrets of Magic|Secrets of Magic]] 
**Ability Type** Familiar
This ability is for a shadow familiar. A [[DATABASE/archetype/Shadowcaster|shadowcaster]], however, can select this ability for any kind of familiar. Your familiar gains the Shadow Step action. You must be at least 7th level to select this familiar ability for your familiar. 
 **Shadow Step** <span class="action-icon">1</span> (conjuration, shadow, teleportation) **Requirements** The familiar is in dim light or darkness. **Effect** The familiar teleports itself up to 30 feet. The destination must be in dim light or darkness and must be within your familiar's line of sight and line of effect. This action has the trait matching your tradition of magic, or occult if you aren't a spellcaster.
