# Fervent Spell<span class="item-type">Major Gift</span>

<span class="item-trait">Emotion</span><span class="item-trait">Enchantment</span><span class="item-trait">Mental</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]]

---
While the relic's fervor gift lasts, you have access to the spell provided by the emotional state, which you can cast using your own spell slots. You also have access to the following activation.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> command, Interact ; **Effect** The relic Casts the Spell associated with its [[DATABASE/archetype/Cathartic Mage|emotional fervor]] at the spell's lowest level.
