**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, Interact ; **Effect** Pollen and poison spores spray from your relic, dealing 1d6 poison damage for every 2 relic levels (minimum 1d6) to all creatures in a 15-foot cone (basic Fortitude save).
