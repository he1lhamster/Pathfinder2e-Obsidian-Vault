**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You breathe out a cone or line of energy. You deal 2d6 damage for every 2 levels of the soul seed to all creatures in either a 30-foot cone or an 80-foot line, with a basic Reflex save. The shape is whichever is appropriate for the soul seed's draconic origin, as is the damage type. This ability has the trait matching the damage type.
