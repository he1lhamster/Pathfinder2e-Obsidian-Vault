<span class="item-trait">Beneficial</span><span class="item-trait">Fortune</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Kingmaker Adventure Path|Kingmaker Adventure Path]]
Good weather raises spirits among citizens.
**Kingdom Skill** [[DATABASE/skill/Wilderness|Wilderness]]; **Leader** Magister
**Critical Success** The glorious climate puts everyone in an excellent mood. Reduce Unrest by 1d6, and once during your next Kingdom turn when you attempt an [[DATABASE/skill/Agriculture|Agriculture]], [[DATABASE/skill/Boating|Boating]], [[DATABASE/skill/Exploration|Exploration]], or Wilderness check, you can roll twice and take the better result.
**Success** Extended good weather makes life pleasant and easy. Reduce Unrest by 1d4.
**Failure** Good weather produces a short but pleasant respite. Reduce Unrest by 1.
**Critical Failure** The good weather doesn't last long enough to help.
