# Facetbound Cascader

Facetbound cascaders must have been arcane spellcasters in life; in undeath, they draw upon the power of embedded [[DATABASE/equipment/Aeon Stone|orange prism aeon stone]] to defend themselves from magical damage and redirect that energy toward their opponents. Despite their incredible intelligence, facetbound cascaders have few memories of their former lives and typically serve the whims of their creator.
