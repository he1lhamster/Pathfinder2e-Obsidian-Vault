# Vault Builder

Vault builders still possess the full power [[DATABASE/deity/Sairazul|Sairazul]] instilled in them, capable of creating new life and shaping the world around them. A vault builder's life span is several millennia, but their memories are preserved within a crystalline generation stone when they die to be placed inside a newly constructed vault builder body.
