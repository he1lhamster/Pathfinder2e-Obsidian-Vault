###  Vincuvicar Thrones

Each vincuvicar possesses a personal, spiked iron throne—a symbol of authority. This iron throne is an object with Hardness 20 and 500 Hit Points. If a vincuvicar's throne is destroyed, the velstrac must return to the [[DATABASE/plane/Shadow Plane|Shadow Plane]] to acquire or construct a replacement.
