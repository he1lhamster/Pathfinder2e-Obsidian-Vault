# Nyogoth

Little more than a coiled mass of intestines encircling a massive gaping maw, this qlippoth is an [[DATABASE/plane/Abyss|Abyssal]] scavenger, subsisting on the filth and leftovers of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Demon|demons]] and qlippoth alike, although it relishes any opportunity to consume living prey. Despite this seeming lowly role in the Abyssal ecosystem, a nyogoth is far from a stupid beast and can orchestrate cunning ambushes to secure its next meal.
