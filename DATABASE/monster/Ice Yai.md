# Ice Yai

Ice yai combine the brutality of [[DATABASE/monster/Frost Giant|frost giants]] with the grace of martial artists. More than any other oni, ice yai delight not in the pleasures of the flesh, but in flesh itself—rather than indulging in oft-destructive mortal vices like drink or lust, ice yai pursue physical perfection, reveling in the process of turning their bodies into powerful fighting machines.
