# Urdefhan Tormentor

Urdefhan spellcasters with a knack for the divine are invariably unholy worshippers of daemonkind, typically taking on one of [[DATABASE/plane/Abaddon|Abaddon]]'s Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse as their patron deity. With their unholy gifts, these urdefhan tormentors summon [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Daemon|daemons]] into battle and bolster their allies with profane magic, preferring to stick to the sidelines rather than enter the fray directly.
