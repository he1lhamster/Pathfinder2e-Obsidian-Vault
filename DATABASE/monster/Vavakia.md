###  Saurian Tyrants

Vavakias carve out tyrannical empires throughout the [[DATABASE/plane/Abyss|Abyss]], particularly in steaming jungles and fetid swamps. Their minions are usually lesser demons, [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Xulgath|xulgaths]], or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Saurian|saurians]]. They enjoy coming to the [[DATABASE/plane/Material Plane|Material Plane]] to feed on souls and spread devastation.
