###  Necropoisons

Many virulak necromancers seek to find or recreate the fabled “necropoisons,” or toxins that work on undead despite their immunity to poison. Rumored to be a magical combination of positive energy and venom from magical creatures, necropoisons have eluded even the most talented virulaks—so far.
