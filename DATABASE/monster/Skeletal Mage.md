# Skeletal Mage

Rarely, the bones of a spellcaster can be used to create a skeleton that retains a limited ability to cast spells. These spells are almost always offensive in nature, and the skeletal mage uses them with reckless abandon. Skeletal mages are often misidentified as [[Lich]], and while not nearly as cunning or deadly, they still pose a significant threat to their foes.
