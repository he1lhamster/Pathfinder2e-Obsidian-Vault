More future memories of time magic percolate back to the present, teaching you spells you've yet to learn. Add [[DATABASE/spell/Behold the Weave|behold the weave]], [[DATABASE/spell/Cast into Time|cast into time]], [[DATABASE/spell/Haste|haste]], [[DATABASE/spell/Loose Time's Arrow|loose time's arrow]], [[DATABASE/spell/Quicken Time|quicken time]], [[DATABASE/spell/Slow|slow]], and [[DATABASE/spell/Stagnate Time|stagnate time]] to your spell list. You can thus potentially learn these spells even if they aren't normally on your tradition's spell list.
