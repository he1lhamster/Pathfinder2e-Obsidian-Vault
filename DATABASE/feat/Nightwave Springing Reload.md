# Nightwave Springing Reload <span class="action-icon">1</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 6</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Gunslinger</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Firebrands|Firebrands]]
**Access** Members of the Firebrands at the rank of second mark or higher have access to this feat.

---
Training with the crew of the Nightwave has taught you their technique for reloading while leaping to board another ship. You [[DATABASE/action/Leap|Leap]] and then Interact to reload, or Interact to reload and then Leap. You can perform this ability as a 2-action activity to use your Leap to [[DATABASE/action/High Jump|High Jump]] or [[DATABASE/action/Long Jump|Long Jump]] without needing to Stride at least 10 feet first.
