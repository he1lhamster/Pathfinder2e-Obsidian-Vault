# Signature Synergy <span class="item-type">Feat 14</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Secrets of Magic|Secrets of Magic]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Summoner|Summoner]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Advanced Synergy|Advanced Synergy]]

---
Your eidolon gains an evolution integral to its form, and it comes more easily than your other synergies. You gain one evolution feat from the following list: [[DATABASE/feat/Airborne Form|Airborne Form]], [[DATABASE/feat/Burrowing Form|Burrowing Form]], [[DATABASE/feat/Ever-Vigilant Senses|Ever-Vigilant Senses]], or [[DATABASE/feat/Hulking Size|Hulking Size]]. For the purpose of meeting its prerequisites, your summoner level is equal to your character level – 4.
