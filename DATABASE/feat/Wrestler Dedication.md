# Wrestler Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Grand Bazaar|Grand Bazaar]]
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Wrestler|Wrestler]]
**Prerequisites** trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]]; trained in unarmed attacks, and unarmored defense

---
Your training in the wrestling arts has made you particularly adept at moving, striking, and grappling while unencumbered. You become an expert in Athletics and gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Titan Wrestler|Titan Wrestler]] skill feat. You don't take the –2 circumstance penalty for making a lethal attack with your nonlethal unarmed attacks. In addition, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Fortitude DC when resisting an opponent's attempts to [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grapple]] you or [[DATABASE/monsterability/Swallow Whole|Swallow you Whole]].
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the wrestler archetype.
