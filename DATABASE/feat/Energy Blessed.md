# Energy Blessed <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Character Guide|Character Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/heritage/Elemental Heart Dwarf|Elemental Heart Dwarf]]

---
Energy runs through your blood more powerfully. When you use [[DATABASE/action/Energy Emanation|Energy Emanation]], you can create an emanation of 5 feet, 10 feet, or 15 feet. The damage increases to 6d6 plus an additional 1d6 for every level you have above 9th, instead of 1d6 plus an additional 1d6 for every 2 levels above 1st.

## Traits

**Dwarf:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have [[DATABASE/monsterability/Darkvision|darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
