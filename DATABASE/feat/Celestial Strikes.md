# Celestial Strikes <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

Your connection to good arms all your attacks against forces of evil. All your weapon and unarmed Strikes deal 1 additional good damage and have the good and magical traits.

## Traits

**Aasimar:**

A creature with this trait has the aasimar versatile heritage. Aasimars are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from celestial beings. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by aasimars.
