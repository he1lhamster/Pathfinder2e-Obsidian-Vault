# Well-Groomed <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Catfolk</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You are fastidious about keeping yourself clean, whether licking your fur or carefully using traditional catfolk hygiene products, to salubrious effect. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against diseases. If you roll a success on a saving throw against a disease, you get a critical success instead.

## Traits

**Catfolk:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Catfolk|catfolk]] ancestry. Catfolk are humanoids with feline features. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by catfolk. An item with this trait is created and used by catfolk.
