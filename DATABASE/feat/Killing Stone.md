# Killing Stone <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Kitsune</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Star Orb|Star Orb]]

---
Your star orb hides a deadly miasma. You can cast 5th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Cloudkill|cloudkill]] as a divine innate spell once per day. The spell's normal components are replaced with a focus component (your star orb) and a somatic component to aim the orb towards the location for the spell.

## Traits

**Kitsune:**

Shapeshifting humanoids whose true forms resemble foxes.
