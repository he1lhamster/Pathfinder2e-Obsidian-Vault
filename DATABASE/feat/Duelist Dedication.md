# Duelist Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Duelist|Duelist]]
**Prerequisites** trained in light armor and simple weapons

---
You are always ready to draw your weapon and begin a duel, no matter the circumstances. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Quick Draw|Quick Draw]] ranger feat, enabling you to both draw and attack with a weapon as 1 action. This serves as Quick Draw for the purpose of meeting prerequisites.
**Special** You cannot select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the duelist archetype.
