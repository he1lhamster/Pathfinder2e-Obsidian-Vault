Your quick gestures and flair for performance distract your opponent, leaving it vulnerable to your follow-up attacks. You [[DATABASE/action/Feint|Feint]] against an opponent within the required weapon's first range increment, rather than an opponent within melee reach. If you succeed, the foe is [[DATABASE/condition/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]] against your melee and ranged attacks, rather than only your melee attacks. On a critical failure, you're flat-footed against the target's melee and ranged attacks, rather than only its melee attacks.
