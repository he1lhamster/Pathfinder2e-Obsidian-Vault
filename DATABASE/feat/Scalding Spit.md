# Scalding Spit <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Goblin</span>
**Source** [[Character Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[Torch Goblin]]

---
Your bodily fluids burn with surprising volatility, as if you ran on oil instead of blood. As long as you are taking [[Persistent Damage]], you gain a boiling spit ranged unarmed attack with a range of 30 feet that deals 1d6 fire damage.

## Traits

**Goblin:**

A creature with this trait can be one of several kinds of creature, including [[Goblin]], [[Hobgoblin]], and [[Bugbear]]. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
