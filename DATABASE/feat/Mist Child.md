# Mist Child <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You blend in with unnatural subtlety. When you are [[DATABASE/condition/Concealed|concealed]] or [[DATABASE/condition/Hidden|hidden]], increase the DC of the flat check to target you to 6 if you're concealed or 12 if you're hidden.

## Traits

**Changeling:**

A creature with this trait has the changeling versatile heritage. Changelings are the children of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Hag|hags]] and members of other humanoid ancestries. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by changelings.
