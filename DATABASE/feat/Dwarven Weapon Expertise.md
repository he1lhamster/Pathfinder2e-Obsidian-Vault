# Dwarven Weapon Expertise <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Dwarf</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Dwarven Weapon Familiarity|Dwarven Weapon Familiarity]]

---
Your dwarven affinity blends with your training, granting you great skill with dwarven weapons. Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in certain weapons, you also gain that proficiency for [[DATABASE/weapon/Battle Axe|battle axes]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Pick|picks]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Warhammer|warhammers]], and all dwarven weapons in which you are trained.

## Traits

**Dwarf:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have [[DATABASE/monsterability/Darkvision|darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
