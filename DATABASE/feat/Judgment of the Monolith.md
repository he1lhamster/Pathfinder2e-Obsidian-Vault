# Judgment of the Monolith <span class="item-type">Feat 12</span>

You can use the oaths bound in your _ka stone_ to compel the truth from the living and the dead. You can activate your _ka stone_ to target a single living creature, causing that creature to experience the effects of being in a [[DATABASE/spell/Zone of Truth|zone of truth]]. You can also activate it with a 10-minute activation (envision) to cast [[DATABASE/spell/Talking Corpse|talking corpse]].
