# Reclaim Spell <span class="action-icon">1</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 20</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Wizard</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 156. The Apocalypse Prophet|Pathfinder #156: The Apocalypse Prophet]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Reprepare Spell|Reprepare Spell]]

---
You have learned to manipulate reflections of magic, similar to the _resonant reflections_. You reclaim the magic of an ongoing spell, using it reprepare a spell with a duration. You Dismiss a Spell of 4th level or lower that you cast today from a spell slot with at least half its duration remaining, even if that spell can't normally be Dismissed. You can then spend 10 minutes to reprepare the spell in that spell slot, as [[DATABASE/feat/Reprepare Spell|Reprepare Spell]].
