# Dream Magic <span class="item-type">Feat 8</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span>
**Source** [[Dark Archive]]
**Archetype** [[Sleepwalker]]
**Prerequisites** [[Sleepwalker Dedication]]

---
You learn dream-related magic to aid your studies. Choose [[Dream Message]] or [[Sleep]] upon taking this feat; you learn this spell as a 4th-level innate occult spell. If you choose _sleep_, you can cast the spell only while in a [[Sleepwalker Dedication]]. You become trained in occult spell attack rolls and spell DCs, and your spellcasting ability for these spells is Wisdom.
**Special** You can take this feat twice, gaining the spell you didn't select initially the second time.
