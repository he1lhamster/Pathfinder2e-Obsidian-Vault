# Wild Strider <span class="item-type">Feat 12</span>

**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[Horizon Walker]]
**Prerequisites** [[Horizon Walker Dedication]]

Natural obstacles pose no obstacle to you. You gain the ranger's [[Ranger]] class feature. This allows you to ignore the effects of all non-magical difficult terrain, treat greater difficult terrain as merely difficult terrain, and gives you an additional benefit from Favored Terrain based on the terrain.
