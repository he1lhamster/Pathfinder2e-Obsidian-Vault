# Mature Trained Companion <span class="item-type">Feat 6</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Archetype</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 152. Legacy of the Lost God|Pathfinder #152: Legacy of the Lost God]]
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Animal Trainer|Animal Trainer
]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Animal Trainer Dedication|Animal Trainer Dedication]]

---
Your animal companion grows up, becoming a mature animal companion. Increase its proficiency rank in [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]] to expert instead of one of the three skill increases for mature. It gains the Mesmerizing Performance advanced maneuver, rather than the normal advanced maneuver for its type.

### [[DATABASE/action/Mesmerizing Performance|Mesmerizing Performance
]] <span class="action-icon">1</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Concentrate</span><span class="item-trait">Emotion</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 152. Legacy of the Lost God|Pathfinder #152: Legacy of the Lost God]]
**Requirements** The companion’s last action was a successful [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]] check to [[DATABASE/action/Perform|Perform]].

---
The companion maintains its performance to captivate a single target within 30 feet that witnessed its successful performance. The target must attempt a Will save.
**Success** The target is unaffected and temporarily immune for 1 hour
**Failure** The target is [[DATABASE/condition/Fascinated|fascinated]] by the companion for its next action and then is temporarily immune for 1 hour.
**Critical Failure** The target is fascinated by the companion for 1 round. While it remains fascinated, it can’t use reactions.
