# Sudden Mindfulness <span class="action-icon">5</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Vanara</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Impossible Lands|Impossible Lands]]
**Trigger** You roll a success on a saving throw against an effect that would make you [[DATABASE/condition/Fascinated|fascinated]] or [[DATABASE/condition/Dazzled|dazzled]].

---
Your mind retains full awareness despite the best attempts of others, and your focus throws others off-balance. You get a critical success on the triggering saving throw, and if the triggering effect was caused by a creature, that creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]] to you until the end of your next turn.

## Traits

**Vanara:**

Vanaras are monkeylike humanoids who share a devotion to monastic training.
