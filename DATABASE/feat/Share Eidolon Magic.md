# Share Eidolon Magic <span class="item-type">Feat 14</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Secrets of Magic|Secrets of Magic]]

Your eidolon shares innate spells with you. You can cast the innate spells your eidolon gained from Magical Understudy, [[DATABASE/feat/Magical Adept|Magical Adept]], and [[DATABASE/feat/Magical Master|Magical Master]]. You and your eidolon share the same daily uses of these innate spells.
