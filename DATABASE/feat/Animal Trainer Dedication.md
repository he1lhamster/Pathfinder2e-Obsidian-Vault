# Animal Trainer Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Animal Trainer|Animal Trainer
]]
**Prerequisites** trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Nature|Nature]]

---
You gain the services of a young animal companion that travels with you and obeys simple commands as best as it can. This trained animal is trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]] instead of the skill listed for its type. 
**Special** You can’t select another dedication feat until you’ve gained two other feats from the animal trainer archetype.
