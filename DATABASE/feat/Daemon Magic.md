The magic of Abaddon runs through your blood, and you can wield that power. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Death Knell|death knell]] and [[DATABASE/spell/False Life|false life]] each once per day as 2nd-level divine innate spells.

## Traits

**Tiefling:**

A creature with this trait has the tiefling versatile heritage. Tieflings are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from fiends. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by tieflings.
