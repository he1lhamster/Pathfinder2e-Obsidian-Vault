# Linguist Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Linguist|Linguist]]
**Prerequisites** you speak at least three languages

---
You have studied languages and their development. You become trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Society|Society]]; if you were already trained in Society, you instead become an expert in Society. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Multilingual|Multilingual]] skill feat twice.
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the linguist archetype.
