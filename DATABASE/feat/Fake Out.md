With a skilled flourish of your weapon, you force an enemy to acknowledge you as a threat. Make an attack roll to [[DATABASE/action/Aid|Aid]] the triggering attack. If you dealt damage to that enemy with the same weapon since the start of your last turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to this roll.
