You are particularly skilled at using a shield in combat. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Reactive Shield|Reactive Shield]] fighter feat. This fulfills any prerequisite requiring Reactive Shield as normal.
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the bastion archetype.
