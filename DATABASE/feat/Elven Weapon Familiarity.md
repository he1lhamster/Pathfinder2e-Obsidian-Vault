# Elven Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

You favor bows and other elegant weapons. You are trained with [[DATABASE/weapon/Longbow|longbows]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Composite Longbow|composite longbows]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Longsword|longswords]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Rapier|rapiers]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Shortbow|shortbows]], and [[DATABASE/weapon/Composite Shortbow|composite shortbows]].
 In addition, you gain access to all uncommon elf weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial elf weapons are simple weapons and advanced elf weapons are martial weapons.
