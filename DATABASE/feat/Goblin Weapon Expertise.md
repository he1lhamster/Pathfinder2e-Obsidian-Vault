# Goblin Weapon Expertise <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Goblin</span>
**Source** [[Core Rulebook]] 
**Prerequisites** [[Goblin Weapon Familiarity]]

---
Your goblin affinity blends with your class training, granting you great skill with goblin weapons. Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in a given weapon or weapons, you also gain that proficiency in the [[Dogslicer]], [[Horsechopper]], and all goblin weapons in which you are trained.

## Traits

**Goblin:**

A creature with this trait can be one of several kinds of creature, including [[Goblin]], [[Hobgoblin]], and [[Bugbear]]. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
