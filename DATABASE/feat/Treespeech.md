# Treespeech <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Ardande</span>
**Source** [[Rage of Elements]]
**Prerequisites** You speak [[Muan]].

---
Your connection to elemental wood allows you to communicate with trees in the [[Material Plane]] in Muan, the language of wood elementals. You can ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the [[Diplomacy]] skill with woody plants like trees and shrubs.

## Traits

**Ardande:**

Ardandes are [[Planar Scion]] descended from [[Kizidhar]] and other beings of elemental wood.
