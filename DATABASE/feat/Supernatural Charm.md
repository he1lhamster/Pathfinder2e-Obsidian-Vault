# Supernatural Charm <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

The elven magic in your blood manifests as a force you can use to become more appealing or alluring. You can cast 1st-level [[DATABASE/spell/Charm|charm]] as an arcane innate spell once per day.

## Traits

**Half-Elf:**

A creature with this trait is part [[DATABASE/ancestry/Human|human]] and part [[DATABASE/ancestry/Elf|elf]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by half-elves.
