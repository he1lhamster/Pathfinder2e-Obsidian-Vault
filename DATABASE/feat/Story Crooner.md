# Story Crooner <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Strix</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
You're a talented story weaver and use your voice effectively. You are trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]]. If you would automatically become trained in Performance, you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You also gain the Impressive Performance skill feat and gain a +1 circumstance bonus when Performing for an audience of strix.

## Traits

**Strix:**

Winged humanoids, most of whom live in and around Cheliax.
