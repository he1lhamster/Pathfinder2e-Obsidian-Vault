# Loud Singer <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Goblin</span>
**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[Goblin Song]]

---
Staying on pitch, proper breath control, and remembering the words are all less important than the real measure of a good singer: volume! The range of your [[Goblin Song]] is increased to 60 feet, and you can target one additional enemy when you use it.

## Traits

**Goblin:**

A creature with this trait can be one of several kinds of creature, including [[Goblin]], [[Hobgoblin]], and [[Bugbear]]. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
