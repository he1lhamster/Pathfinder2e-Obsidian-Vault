# Catrina's Presence <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Duskwalker</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
You've adopted the calming presence of [[DATABASE/monster/Catrina|catrinas]], the festive dead; you might have even danced with a catrina yourself! So long as you are visibly carrying fresh flowers or colorful ribbons somewhere on your person, you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Calm Emotions|calm emotions]] twice per day as a 2nd-level divine innate spell.

## Traits

**Duskwalker:**

A creature with this trait has the duskwalker versatile heritage. Duskwalkers are planar scions infused with the supernatural energy of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Psychopomp|psychopomps]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by duskwalkers.
