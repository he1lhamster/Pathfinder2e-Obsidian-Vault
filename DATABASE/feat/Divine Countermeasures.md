# Divine Countermeasures <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Aasimar</span>
**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You've studied your celestial heritage with the intent of better defending yourself, and you've found that your techniques are equally powerful against celestials, fiends, and other divine entities. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to all saving throws against divine effects.

## Traits

**Aasimar:**

A creature with this trait has the aasimar versatile heritage. Aasimars are [[Planar Scion]] descended from celestial beings. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by aasimars.
