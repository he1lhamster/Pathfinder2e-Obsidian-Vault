# Makeshift Strike <span class="action-icon">1</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 8</span>

**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Weapon Improviser Dedication|Weapon Improviser Dedication]]

---
You quickly snatch up whatever's nearby and then attack with it as an improvised weapon. You Interact to pick up a nearby object you could use as an improvised weapon. It must be located at around your chest level, such as a mug on a table, rather than an object on the floor. You then either Strike or make an [[DATABASE/feat/Improvised Pummel|Improvised Pummel]] with the improvised weapon.
