# Halfling Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Halfling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]]

---
You favor traditional halfling weapons, so you've learned how to use them more effectively. You have the trained proficiency with the [[DATABASE/weapon/Frying Pan|frying pan]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Sling|sling]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Halfling Sling Staff|halfling sling staff]], and [[DATABASE/weapon/Shortsword|shortsword]]. You gain access to all uncommon halfling weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial halfling weapons are simple weapons and advanced halfling weapons are martial weapons.
