# Halfling Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Halfling</span>
**Source** [[Core Rulebook]]

---
You favor traditional halfling weapons, so you've learned how to use them more effectively. You have the trained proficiency with the [[Frying Pan]], [[Sling]], [[Halfling Sling Staff]], and [[Shortsword]]. You gain access to all uncommon halfling weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial halfling weapons are simple weapons and advanced halfling weapons are martial weapons.
