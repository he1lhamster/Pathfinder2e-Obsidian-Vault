## Traits

**Aasimar:**

A creature with this trait has the aasimar versatile heritage. Aasimars are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from celestial beings. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by aasimars.

**Lineage:**

A feat with this trait indicates a character's descendance from a particular type of creature. You can have only one lineage feat. You can select a lineage feat only at 1st level, and you can't retrain into or out of these feats.
