# Forgotten Presence <span class="item-type">Feat 8</span>

The world conspires to keep you forgotten and unnoticed— memories cloud, ink stains out your name in ledgers, and portraits of you crack and warp over your face. Characters attempting to [[DATABASE/action/Recall Knowledge|Recall Knowledge]] about you take a –2 circumstance penalty. Add [[DATABASE/spell/Modify Memory|modify memory]] to your spell list as a primal spell.
