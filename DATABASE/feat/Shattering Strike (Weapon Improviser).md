**Trigger** An improvised weapon that you are wielding becomes broken as a result of a critical success on an Improvised Pummel.

---
The improvised weapon shatters, destroying it completely but dealing an additional 3d6 piercing damage to the creature you hit with the [[DATABASE/feat/Improvised Pummel|Improvised Pummel]]. This extra damage you apply on the critical hit doesn't double from the critical hit, as normal.
