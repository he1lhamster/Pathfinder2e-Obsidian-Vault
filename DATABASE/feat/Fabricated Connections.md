# Fabricated Connections <span class="item-type">Feat 7</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Skill</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Dandy|Dandy]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Dandy Dedication|Dandy Dedication]]; master in [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]]

---
You can lie about having taken an action so convincingly that you acquire all the tangible benefits of having successfully completed it. You can roll [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]] in place of a different skill when attempting to [[DATABASE/action/Earn Income|Earn Income]], [[DATABASE/action/Make an Impression|Make an Impression]], [[DATABASE/action/Request|Request]], or [[DATABASE/action/Subsist|Subsist]]. You can use Fabricated Connections to Make an Impression or Request once per day, and you can also use it once per week to Earn Income or Subsist.

## Traits

**Archetype:**

This feat belongs to an archetype.

**Skill:**

A general feat with the skill trait improves your skills and their actions or gives you new actions for a skill. A feat with this trait can be selected when a class grants a skill feat or general feat. Archetype feats with the skill trait can be selected in place of a skill feat if you have that archetype's dedication feat.
