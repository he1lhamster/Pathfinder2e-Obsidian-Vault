# Look but Don't Touch <span class="action-icon">2</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Impossible Lands|Impossible Lands]]
**Frequency** once per day

---
Many flowers are beautiful to look upon but deadly to touch—and you're one of them. For 1 minute, any creature that touches you or damages you with a melee weapon without the reach trait or with a melee unarmed attack takes 3d6 poison damage.

## Traits

**Ghoran:**

Ghorans are sapient plants grown in the shape of humanoids with floral faces.

**Poison:**

An effect with this trait delivers a poison or deals poison damage. An item with this trait is poisonous and might cause an affliction.
