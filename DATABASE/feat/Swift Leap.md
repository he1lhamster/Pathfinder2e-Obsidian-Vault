Your undead physiology allows you to leap quickly toward or away from your opponent. You [[DATABASE/action/Leap|Leap]]. This movement doesn't trigger reactions.
 If you are satiated, you can choose to end your satiation to instead [[DATABASE/action/High Jump|High Jump]] or [[DATABASE/action/Long Jump|Long Jump]] without triggering reactions, as you take a quick, enormous jump before any foe can react to your movement.
