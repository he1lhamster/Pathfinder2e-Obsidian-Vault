# Vishkanya Weapon Expertise <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Vishkanya</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Impossible Lands|Impossible Lands]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Vishkanya Weapon Familiarity|Vishkanya Weapon Familiarity]]

---
Extensive training has granted you an elegant control in the use of vishkanyan weapons. Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in a given weapon or weapons, you also gain that proficiency in the [[DATABASE/weapon/Blowgun|blowgun]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Fighting Fan|fighting fan]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Kris|kris]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Kukri|kukri]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Shuriken|shuriken]], and all vishkanyan weapons in which you are trained.

## Traits

**Vishkanya:**

These faintly ophidian humanoids have venomous blood and saliva.
