You squeeze your opponent in a vicious bear hug, putting intense pressure on their nerves, joints, or other pain points. Attempt an [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] check to [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grapple]] the creature you have grabbed or restrained, with the following effects instead of the usual effects.
**Critical Success** The target is [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy 2]] until the end of its next turn and clumsy 1 for 1 minute.
**Success** The target is clumsy 1 until the end of its next turn.
