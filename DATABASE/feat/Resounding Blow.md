You strike your enemy in the head with such force that their ears ring. Make a melee Strike. If the Strike hits and deals damage, the target is [[DATABASE/condition/Deafened|deafened]] until the start of your next turn (or for 1 minute on a critical hit).

## Traits

**Rage:**

You must be raging to use abilities with the rage trait, and they end automatically when you stop raging.
