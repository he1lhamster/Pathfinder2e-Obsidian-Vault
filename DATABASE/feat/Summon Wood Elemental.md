# Summon Wood Elemental <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Ardande</span>
**Source** [[Rage of Elements]]

---
Your connection to the [[Plane of Wood]] allows you to summon elemental allies. Once per day, you can cast [[Summon Elemental]] as a 5th-rank primal innate spell, but the elemental must be a [[Elemental, Wood]].

## Traits

**Ardande:**

Ardandes are [[Planar Scion]] descended from [[Kizidhar]] and other beings of elemental wood.
