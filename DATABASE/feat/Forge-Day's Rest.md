Your unusual rest cycle allows you to recover faster. As long as you rest for 12 hours, you gain the effects of the [[DATABASE/feat/Fast Recovery|Fast Recovery]] general feat and you can go 20 hours without resting before becoming [[DATABASE/condition/Fatigued|fatigued]].
