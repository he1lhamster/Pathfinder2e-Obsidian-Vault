You achieve a state of perfect calm before unleashing your attack, opening your mind to the minute movements of the world around you to increase your accuracy. Make a ranged weapon Strike against a target within your weapon's first range increment. This Strike ignores the target's [[DATABASE/condition/Concealed|concealed]] condition and all cover.
