While sharing a creature's space using Dance Underfoot, your weapons and unarmed attacks gain the trip trait, but only against the creature whose space you share. You can be in the same space as a Large or larger [[DATABASE/condition/Prone|prone]] creature, even if it's not your ally.

## Traits

**Halfling:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Halfling|halfling]] ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
