# Horizon Walker Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Horizon Walker|Horizon Walker]]
**Prerequisites** trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Survival|Survival]]

---
You've mastered travel within a specific type of terrain. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Favored Terrain|Favored Terrain]] feat. When in your favored terrain, you gain a +10-foot circumstance bonus to your travel Speed. When other creatures [[DATABASE/action/Follow the Expert|Follow the Expert]] with you as a guide in your favored terrain, they gain both the ability to ignore non-magical difficult terrain for the purpose of their travel Speed and the bonus to travel Speed.
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the horizon walker archetype.
