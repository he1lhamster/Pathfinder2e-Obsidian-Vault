You've trained with weapons used by your genie ancestors. You are trained with [[DATABASE/weapon/Falchion|falchions]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Ranseur|ranseurs]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Scimitar|scimitars]], and [[DATABASE/weapon/Trident|tridents]]. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon geniekin weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial geniekin weapons are simple weapons and advanced geniekin weapons are martial weapons.
