You've trained with weapons used by your genie ancestors. You are trained with [[Falchion]], [[Ranseur]], [[Scimitar]], and [[Trident]]. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon geniekin weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial geniekin weapons are simple weapons and advanced geniekin weapons are martial weapons.
