# Animal Speaker <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Sprite</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** ability to communicate with some type of animals (such as bats or arthropods)

---
You can speak to all animals, not just a particular subset. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/action/Make an Impression|Make an Impression]] on animals (which usually uses the [[DATABASE/skill/Diplomacy|Diplomacy]] skill).

## Traits

**Sprite:**

A family of diminutive winged fey with a strong connection to primal magic.
