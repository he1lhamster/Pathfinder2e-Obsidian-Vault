# Advanced School Spell <span class="item-type">Feat 8</span>

<span class="item-trait">Wizard</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/class_/Wizard|arcane school]]

---
You gain access to a powerful new school spell depending on your arcane school. If you’re an abjurer, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Energy Absorption|energy absorption]]; if you’re a conjurer, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Dimensional Steps|dimensional steps]]; if you’re a diviner, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Vigilant Eye|vigilant eye]]; if you’re an enchanter, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Dread Aura|dread aura]]; if you’re an evoker, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Elemental Tempest|elemental tempest]]; if you’re an illusionist, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Invisibility Cloak|invisibility cloak]]; if you’re a necromancer, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Life Siphon|life siphon]]; and if you’re a transmuter, you gain [[DATABASE/spell/Shifting Form|shifting form]]. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.
