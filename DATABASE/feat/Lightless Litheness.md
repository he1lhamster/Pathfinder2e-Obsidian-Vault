# Lightless Litheness <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Fetchling</span>
**Source** [[Ancestry Guide]]

---
Your body is almost as flexible as your shadow. When you critically fail at [[Squeeze]], you get a failure instead. Additionally, when you roll a success at an [[Escape]] check, you get a critical success instead; if you roll a critical success, you can Step instead of Striding up to 5 feet.

## Traits

**Fetchling:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the fetchling ancestry. Fetchlings were once human, but generations of life on the [[Shadow Plane]] have transformed them.
