**Prerequisites** member of the Knights of Lastwall; [[DATABASE/feat/Shield Block|Shield Block]]
**Access** You are from the Eye of Dread region.

---
You’ve sworn yourself as a sentry for the Knights of Lastwall, the first line of defense against incursions by the Whispering Tyrant throughout Golarion. You become trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] and in Undead [[DATABASE/skill/Lore|Lore]]; if you were already trained, you become an expert instead. Finally, you gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Reactive Shield|Reactive Shield]] fighter feat.
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the Lastwall sentry archetype.
