# Elemental Trade <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Oread</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
You have a strong connection with stone and metal. You gain the trained proficiency rank in [[DATABASE/skill/Crafting|Crafting]]. If you would automatically become trained in Crafting (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Specialty Crafting|Specialty Crafting]] skill feat for both Stonemasonry and Blacksmithing.

## Traits

**Oread:**

Oreads are planar scions descended from [[DATABASE/monster/Shaitan|shaitans]].
