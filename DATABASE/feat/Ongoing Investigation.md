# Ongoing Investigation <span class="item-type">Feat 4</span>

<span class="item-trait">Investigator</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You're always investigating what's around you, even as you perform other activities. You can move at full Speed while using the [[DATABASE/action/Investigate|Investigate]] exploration activity, and you can use another exploration activity while Investigating.
