# Ceaseless Shadows <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Halfling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Distracting Shadows|Distracting Shadows]]

---
You excel at going unnoticed, especially among a crowd. You no longer need to have cover or be [[DATABASE/condition/Concealed|concealed]] to [[DATABASE/action/Hide|Hide]] or [[DATABASE/action/Sneak|Sneak]]. If you would have lesser cover from creatures, you gain cover and can [[DATABASE/action/Take Cover|Take Cover]], and if you would have cover from creatures, you gain greater cover.

## Traits

**Halfling:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Halfling|halfling]] ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
