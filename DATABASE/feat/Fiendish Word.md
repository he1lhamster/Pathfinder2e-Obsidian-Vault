# Fiendish Word <span class="item-type">Feat 17</span>

<span class="item-trait">Tiefling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You can call forth a blasphemous word from the fiendish realms to punish your foes. Once per day, you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Divine Decree|divine decree]] as a 7th-level divine innate spell. You must choose evil for the spell, and you can Cast the Spell regardless of whether you have a deity or what their alignment is.

## Traits

**Tiefling:**

A creature with this trait has the tiefling versatile heritage. Tieflings are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from fiends. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by tieflings.
