# Shadowy Disguise <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Fetchling</span>
**Source** [[Ancestry Guide]]

---
You wrap yourself in shadow to change your appearance. You can cast [[Illusory Disguise]] as a 1st-level occult innate spell once per day. This spell gains the shadow trait.

## Traits

**Fetchling:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the fetchling ancestry. Fetchlings were once human, but generations of life on the [[Shadow Plane]] have transformed them.
