# Devilish Wiles <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Tiefling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
Traditionally, fiends are exceptionally good at persuading people to do things, even when their targets know it's a bad idea. You're also rather good at it, compelling the weak and weak-willed to your bidding. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Charm|charm]] once per day as a 1st-level divine innate spell.

## Traits

**Tiefling:**

A creature with this trait has the tiefling versatile heritage. Tieflings are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from fiends. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by tieflings.
