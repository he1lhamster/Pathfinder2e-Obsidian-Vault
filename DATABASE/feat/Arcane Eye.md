# Arcane Eye <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Prerequisites** low-light vision

---
Your eye has been magically enhanced to pierce darkness. You gain darkvision.
**Enhancement** Your eye can see [[DATABASE/condition/Invisible|invisible]] creatures in brief spurts. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/See Invisibility|see invisibility]] as an arcane innate spell once per hour.

## Traits

**Automaton:**

Automatons are living constructs sustained by a magical core.
