# Assured Knowledge <span class="item-type">Feat 6</span>

**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/bardmuse/Enigma|enigma muse]]
* This archetype offers Assured Knowledge at a different level than displayed here.

---
You can procure information with confidence. Whenever you [[DATABASE/action/Recall Knowledge|Recall Knowledge]] using any skill (including [[DATABASE/feat/Bardic Lore|Bardic Lore]]), you can forgo rolling your check to instead receive a result of 10 + your proficiency bonus (don't apply any other bonuses, penalties, or modifiers). As long as you are an expert in a skill, you meet the prerequisites for the [[DATABASE/feat/Automatic Knowledge|Automatic Knowledge]] skill feat in that skill, even if you don't have [[DATABASE/feat/Assurance|Assurance]] in that skill.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more Archetypes in addition to the listed Classes. When selected this way, the feat is not considered to have its class traits.
