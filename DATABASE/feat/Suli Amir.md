You have power connected to the powerful jann amirs. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Read Omens|read omens]] and 4th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Invisibility|invisibility]] each once per day as arcane innate spells, and you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Detect Magic|detect magic]] at will as an arcane innate cantrip, heightened to a spell level equal to half your level rounded up.

## Traits

**Suli:**

Sulis are planar scions descended from [[DATABASE/monster/Janni|jann]].
