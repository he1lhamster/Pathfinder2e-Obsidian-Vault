You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Adopted Ancestry|Adopted Ancestry]] feat and gain a 1st level oread ancestry feat as a bonus feat, ignoring the limitations of the lineage trait. When you gain new ancestry feats, you can select oread feats in addition to feats allowed by your ancestry and heritage.
