# Translucent Skin <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

Your body is translucent while underwater. Whenever you are fully submerged in water, you can [[DATABASE/action/Hide|Hide]] from other creatures even if you don't have cover against them and aren't [[DATABASE/condition/Concealed|concealed]] from them.
