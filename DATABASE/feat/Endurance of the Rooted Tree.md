# Endurance of the Rooted Tree <span class="item-type">Feat 20</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Monk</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 156. The Apocalypse Prophet|Pathfinder #156: The Apocalypse Prophet]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Wholeness of Body|Wholeness of Body]]

---
Just as the Vale of Aroden retains light and life despite inhospitable surroundings, you can sustain yourself regardless of your environment. You don't need to eat, drink, or breathe. In addition, you can cast the [[DATABASE/spell/Wholeness of Body|wholeness of body]] ki spell almost effortlessly. You can cast it as a free action that triggers at the start of your turn.
