# Summon Air Elemental <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

You have a connection to the Inner Spheres, allowing you to summon an elemental ally. Once per day, you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Summon Elemental|summon elemental]] as a 5th-level primal innate spell, but the elemental summoned must be an [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Elemental, Air|air elemental]].

Sylphs are planar scions descended from [[DATABASE/monster/Djinni|djinn]].
