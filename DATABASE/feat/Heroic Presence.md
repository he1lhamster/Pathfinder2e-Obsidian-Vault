# Heroic Presence <span class="action-icon">1</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 17</span>

**Frequency** once per day

The blood of heroes courses through your veins, and you inspire your allies to dig deep and find a new level of resolve. You grant up to 10 willing creatures within 30 feet the effects of a 6th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Zealous Conviction|zealous conviction]], though the effect automatically ends on a target if you give that target a command they would normally find repugnant. This action has the auditory trait or visual trait, depending on how you inspire your allies.

## Traits

**Emotion:**

This effect alters a creature's emotions. Effects with this trait always have the mental trait as well. Creatures with special training or that have mechanical or artificial intelligence are immune to emotion effects.

**Human:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Human|human]] ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.

**Mental:**

A mental effect can alter the target's mind. It has no effect on an object or a mindless creature.
