**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
Your magic rivals that of lesser dragons. Choose one 3rd-level spell and one 4th-level spell from those listed for a dragon spellcaster of your draconic exemplar's type (such as [[Paralyze]] and [[Stinking Cloud]] for a [[Dragon, Black]] exemplar). Links to all types of dragons can be found here. You can cast each of these spells once per day as arcane innate spells.

## Traits

**Kobold:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the kobold ancestry. Kobolds are reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [[Darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
