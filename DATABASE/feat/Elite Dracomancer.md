# Elite Dracomancer <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

Your magic rivals that of lesser dragons. Choose one 3rd-level spell and one 4th-level spell from those listed for a dragon spellcaster of your draconic exemplar's type (such as [[DATABASE/spell/Paralyze|paralyze]] and [[DATABASE/spell/Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]] for a [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Black|black dragon]] exemplar). Links to all types of dragons can be found here. You can cast each of these spells once per day as arcane innate spells.

## Traits

**Kobold:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the kobold ancestry. Kobolds are reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [[DATABASE/monsterability/Darkvision|darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
