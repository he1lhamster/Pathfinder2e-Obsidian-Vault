# Resist Ruin <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Duskwalker</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
Your duskwalker heritage allows you to extend protection against negative energy to yourself or an ally in need. You gain resistance 5 to negative energy, and once per day, you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Death Ward|death ward]] as a divine innate spell.

## Traits

**Duskwalker:**

A creature with this trait has the duskwalker versatile heritage. Duskwalkers are planar scions infused with the supernatural energy of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Psychopomp|psychopomps]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by duskwalkers.
