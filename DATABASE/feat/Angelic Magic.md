You can tap into the magic of angels that runs through your blood. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Humanoid Form|humanoid form]] and [[DATABASE/spell/Remove Fear|remove fear]] each once per day as 2nd-level divine innate spells.

## Traits

**Aasimar:**

A creature with this trait has the aasimar versatile heritage. Aasimars are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from celestial beings. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by aasimars.
