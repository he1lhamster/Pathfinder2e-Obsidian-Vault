# Flourish and Ruin <span class="item-type">Feat 17</span>

<span class="item-trait">Leshy</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
You can call upon the vital essence of your spirit to restore life to your allies and call forth vines to ensnare and bludgeon your foes. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Field of Life|field of life]] and [[DATABASE/spell/Tangling Creepers|tangling creepers]] as 6th-level primal innate spells once per day each. You become an expert in primal spell attack rolls and primal spell DCs.

## Traits

**Leshy:**

Leshies are living plants animated by primal magic.
