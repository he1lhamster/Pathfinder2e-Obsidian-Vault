# Kobold Lore <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Kobold</span>
**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You attentively learned key kobold survival strategies and mythology from your elders. You gain the trained proficiency rank in [[Stealth]] and [[Thievery]]. If you would automatically become trained in one of those skills (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You also become trained in [[Lore]].

## Traits

**Kobold:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the kobold ancestry. Kobolds are reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [[Darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
