# Summon Fiendish Kin <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Tiefling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** any tiefling lineage feat

---
You have a deep connection to the fiendish realms, allowing you to summon a fiend matching your own lineage. Once per day, you can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Summon Fiend|summon fiend]] as a 5th-level divine innate spell. The fiend you summon must match your own lineage.

## Traits

**Tiefling:**

A creature with this trait has the tiefling versatile heritage. Tieflings are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from fiends. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by tieflings.
