# Flinging Blow <span class="action-icon">2</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 16</span>

<span class="item-trait">Monk</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You take a moment to center yourself, then unleash a powerful blow that sends your target tumbling away. Make an unarmed Strike. If you hit, the target must succeed at a Fortitude save against your class DC or be pushed up to 10 feet directly away from you (or 20 feet, on a critical failure) and fall [[DATABASE/condition/Prone|prone]]. If another creature or physical object would prevent this movement, both your target and the blocking creature or object take 1d6 bludgeoning damage per 5 feet of movement that was prevented.
