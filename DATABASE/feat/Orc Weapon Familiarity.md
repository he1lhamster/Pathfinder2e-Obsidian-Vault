# Orc Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Orc</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]]

---
In combat, you favor the brutal weapons that are traditional for your orc ancestors. You are trained with the [[DATABASE/weapon/Falchion|falchion]] and [[DATABASE/weapon/Greataxe|greataxe]]. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon orc weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial orc weapons are simple weapons and advanced orc weapons are martial weapons.
