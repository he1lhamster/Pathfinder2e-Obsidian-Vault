# Stealthy Companion <span class="item-type">Feat 14</span>

<span class="item-trait">Ranger</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Animal Companion|Animal Companion]]; [[DATABASE/feat/Camouflage|Camouflage]]

---
You’ve trained your animal companion to blend in to its surroundings. Your animal companion gains the benefit of the [[DATABASE/feat/Camouflage|Camouflage]] feat. If your companion is a specialized ambusher, its proficiency rank for Stealth increases to master (or legendary if it was already master).
