# Tense Negotiator <span class="item-type">Feat 12</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span>
**Source** [[PFS Guide]]
**Archetype** [[Swordmaster]]
**Prerequisites** [[Swordmaster Dedication]]; master in [[Diplomacy]]

---
You often find that the best way to win a conflict means avoiding a fight in the first place. You're good at talking to potential opponents without making things worse. If you would critically fail at a check to [[Make an Impression]], you instead fail. You can attempt checks to make [[Request]] of creatures who're [[Indifferent]] or [[Unfriendly]] toward you.
