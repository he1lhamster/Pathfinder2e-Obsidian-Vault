# Sinister Appearance <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Ifrit</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
You possess horns, a tail, or red eyes, or could otherwise be mistaken for a [[DATABASE/ancestry/Tiefling|tiefling]]. You gain the trained proficiency rank in [[DATABASE/skill/Intimidation|Intimidation]]. If you would automatically become trained in Intimidation (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Intimidating Glare|Intimidating Glare]] skill feat, as well as a +2 circumstance bonus on [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Impersonate|Impersonate]] when pretending to be a version of yourself who is a tiefling.

## Traits

**Ifrit:**

Ifrits are planar scions descended from [[DATABASE/monster/Efreeti|efreet]].
