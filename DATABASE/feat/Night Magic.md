# Night Magic <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Dhampir</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
You can tap into the magic that runs through your blood. You gain [[DATABASE/spell/Animal Form|animal form]] (wolf only, using the statistics for a canine) and [[DATABASE/spell/Obscuring Mist|obscuring mist]] as 2nd-level divine innate spells. You can cast each of these spells once per day.

## Traits

**Dhampir:**

A creature with this trait has the dhampir versatile heritage. These humanoids are the mortal offspring of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Vampire|vampires]] and members of other ancestries.
