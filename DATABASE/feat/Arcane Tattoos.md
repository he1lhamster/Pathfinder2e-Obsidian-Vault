You have tattoos on your body corresponding to one of the ancient Thassilonian schools of magic. Choose one of the following schools of magic: abjuration ([[DATABASE/spell/Shield|shield]]), conjuration ([[DATABASE/spell/Tanglefoot|tanglefoot]]), enchantment ([[DATABASE/spell/Daze|daze]]), evocation ([[DATABASE/spell/Electric Arc|electric arc]]), illusion ([[DATABASE/spell/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]), necromancy ([[DATABASE/spell/Chill Touch|chill touch]]), or transmutation ([[DATABASE/spell/Sigil|sigil]]). You can cast the associated cantrip (listed in parentheses) as an innate arcane spell at will.
