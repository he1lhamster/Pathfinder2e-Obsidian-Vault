# Archaeologist Dedication <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Archaeologist|Archaeologist]]
**Prerequisites** trained in Perception; trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Society|Society]] and [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]]

---
You are a student of peoples and their histories, and are in constant pursuit of knowledge and artifacts from the past. You become an expert in [[DATABASE/skill/Society|Society]] and [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]], and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/action/Recall Knowledge|Recall Knowledge]] about ancient history, peoples, and cultures.
**Special** You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the archaeologist archetype.
