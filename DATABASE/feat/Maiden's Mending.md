# Maiden's Mending <span class="action-icon">5</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Changeling</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]] 
**Frequency** once per minute
**Trigger** You cast a non-cantrip occult spell or an innate spell from a changeling ancestry feat.

---
You can tap into some of the magic that flows through your blood to give you new life. You gain a number of temporary Hit Points equal to half your level (minimum 1) that last until the end of your next turn.

## Traits

**Changeling:**

A creature with this trait has the changeling versatile heritage. Changelings are the children of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Hag|hags]] and members of other humanoid ancestries. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by changelings.
