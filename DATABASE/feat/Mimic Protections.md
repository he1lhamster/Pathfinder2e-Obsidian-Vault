By adopting _resonant reflections_, you have learned to copy the abilities of other creatures as well. When you use [[DATABASE/action/Hunt Prey|Hunt Prey]] on a foe within 100 feet, you gain resistance 10 to all damage types that target has resistance or immunity to for as long as the foe remains within 100 feet.
