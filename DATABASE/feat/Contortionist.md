# Contortionist <span class="item-type">Feat 4</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Acrobat Dedication|Acrobat Dedication]]

You can squeeze out of tight situations surprisingly quickly, gaining an advantage against foes that try to pin you down. You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Quick Squeeze|Quick Squeeze]] skill feat, and if you're a master in [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]], you can Squeeze at full Speed. Whenever you successfully [[DATABASE/action/Escape|Escape]] using Acrobatics, the creature you Escaped from is [[DATABASE/condition/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]] against the next attack you make against it before the end of your next turn.
