# Aasimar's Mercy <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Aasimar</span>
**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
Your celestial powers allow you to remove lesser afflictions with ease. Each day, you can cast two 4th-level divine innate spells. You can choose from the following spells each time you cast: [[Remove Curse]], [[Remove Disease]], and [[Neutralize Poison]].

## Traits

**Aasimar:**

A creature with this trait has the aasimar versatile heritage. Aasimars are [[Planar Scion]] descended from celestial beings. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by aasimars.
