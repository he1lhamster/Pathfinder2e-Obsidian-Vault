Your constant efforts to augment and perfect your [[DATABASE/equipment/Soul Cage|soul cage]] have bolstered its efficiency. Your _soul cage_ has Hardness equal to your level and HP equal to four times your level. The time it takes your _soul cage_ to rebuild your body is reduced to 1d6 days rather than 1d10 days.
 At 20th level, the time it takes your _soul cage_ to rebuild your body is reduced to 2d12+12 hours.
