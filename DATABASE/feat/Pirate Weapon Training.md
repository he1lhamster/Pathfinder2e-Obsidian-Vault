You're particularly skilled at wielding traditional pirate weapons. You become trained with the following weapons: [[DATABASE/weapon/Hatchet|hatchet]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Rapier|rapier]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Scimitar|scimitar]], and [[DATABASE/weapon/Whip|whip]]. Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency with a given weapon or weapons, you also gain that proficiency rank for these weapons.
