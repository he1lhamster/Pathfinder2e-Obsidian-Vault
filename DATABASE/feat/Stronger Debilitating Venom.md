# Stronger Debilitating Venom <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Vishkanya</span>
**Source** [[Impossible Lands]]
**Prerequisites** [[Debilitating Venom]]; [[Moderate Enhance Venom]]

---
Add one of the following to the debilitations from which you can choose when you [[Envenom]]. These debilitations reduce the damage of all stages as noted.

* **Bungling** (level 13) Reduce damage by 1 die; **Stage 1** no effect; **Stage 2** [[Clumsy]]; **Stage 3** clumsy 2
* **Devitalizing** (level 13) Reduce damage by 3 dice; **Stage 1** no effect; **Stage 2** [[Drained]]; **Stage 3** drained 2
* **Nauseating** (level 13) Reduce damage by 2 dice; **Stage 1** no effect; **Stage 2** [[Sickened]]; **Stage 3** sickened 2
* **Weakening** (level 13) Reduce damage by 1 die; **Stage 1** [[Enfeebled]]; **Stage 2** enfeebled 1; **Stage 3** enfeebled 2

**Special** You can select this feat multiple times, choosing a different debilitation each time.

## Traits

**Vishkanya:**

These faintly ophidian humanoids have venomous blood and saliva.
