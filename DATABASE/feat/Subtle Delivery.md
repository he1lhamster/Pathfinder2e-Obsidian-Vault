# Subtle Delivery <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Alchemist</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Poisoner|Poisoner]]*
* This archetype offers Subtle Delivery at a different level than displayed here.

---
You can capably deliver toxins with a [[DATABASE/weapon/Blowgun|blowgun]]. Your blowgun Strikes can apply injury poisons even if they deal no damage due to a creature's resistance. If you critically succeed at an attack roll using a blowgun loaded with a dart you've poisoned and the target rolls a failure on the poison's initial save, the target critically fails instead.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more Archetypes in addition to the listed Classes. When selected this way, the feat is not considered to have its class traits.
