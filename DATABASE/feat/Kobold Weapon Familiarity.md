# Kobold Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Kobold</span>
**Source** [[Ancestry Guide]]

---
You've trained with weapons ideal for subterranean efficiency. You are trained with the [[Crossbow]], [[Greatpick]], [[Light Pick]], [[Pick]], and [[Spear]]. You also gain access to all uncommon kobold weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial kobold weapons are simple weapons, and advanced kobold weapons are martial weapons.

## Traits

**Kobold:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the kobold ancestry. Kobolds are reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [[Darkvision]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
