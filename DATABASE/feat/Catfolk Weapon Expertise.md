# Catfolk Weapon Expertise <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

Whenever you gain a class feature that grants you expert or greater proficiency in certain weapons, you also gain that proficiency rank in all weapons you are trained in from Catfolk Weapon Familiarity.

## Traits

**Catfolk:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[Catfolk]] ancestry. Catfolk are humanoids with feline features. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by catfolk. An item with this trait is created and used by catfolk.
