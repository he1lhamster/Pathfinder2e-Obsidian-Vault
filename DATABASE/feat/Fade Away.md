# Fade Away <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Halfling</span>
**Source** [[Character Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[Easily Dismissed]]

---
Your ability to blend into the background allows you to fade away entirely or appear innocuous even to magical effects. You gain [[Invisibility]] and [[Misdirection]] as 2nd-level innate occult spells. You can target only yourself with _invisibility_, and you must be the primary target of _misdirection_. You can cast each spell once per day.

## Traits

**Halfling:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[Halfling]] ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
