# Fangs <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Dhampir</span>
**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
Your incisors have grown into true fangs: long, sharp, and well-suited to drawing blood. You gain a fangs unarmed attack that deals 1d6 piercing damage. Your fangs are in the [[Brawling]] group and have the grapple and unarmed traits.

## Traits

**Dhampir:**

A creature with this trait has the dhampir versatile heritage. These humanoids are the mortal offspring of [[Vampire]] and members of other ancestries.
