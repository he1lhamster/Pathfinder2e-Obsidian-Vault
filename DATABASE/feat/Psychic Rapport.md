# Psychic Rapport <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Psychic</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Dark Archive|Dark Archive]]

---
Your telepathic sensitivity intensifies when you're affected by mental magic, making it easier for you to link to other creatures. After you Cast a Spell with the mental trait, until the start of your next turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]], [[DATABASE/skill/Diplomacy|Diplomacy]], and [[DATABASE/skill/Intimidation|Intimidation]] checks, and to Perception checks to [[DATABASE/action/Sense Motive|Sense Motive]].
