# Duskwalker Magic <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Duskwalker</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]]

---
Your connection to psychopomps gives you the power to glimpse the near future and protect corpses from the ravages of undeath. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Augury|augury]] and [[DATABASE/spell/Gentle Repose|gentle repose]] each once per day as 2nd-level divine innate spells.

## Traits

**Duskwalker:**

A creature with this trait has the duskwalker versatile heritage. Duskwalkers are planar scions infused with the supernatural energy of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Psychopomp|psychopomps]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by duskwalkers.
