# Shory Aerialist <span class="item-type">Feat 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Human</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Character Guide|Character Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** Garundi ethnicity, Mauxi ethnicity, Tian-Yae ethnicity, [[DATABASE/feat/Shory Aeromancer|Shory Aeromancer]], or ability to cast [[DATABASE/spell/Fly|fly]]

---
Unique in Golarion’s history, the Shory people developed fighting styles dedicated to combat in the air. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks to Maneuver in Flight and a +5-foot status bonus to your fly Speed whenever you are flying via magic.

## Traits

**Human:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Human|human]] ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
