# Invincible Mutagen <span class="item-type">Feat 12</span>

The fortifying additives you brew into your mutagens make your juggernaut form impervious. Whenever you’re affected by a [[DATABASE/equipment/Juggernaut Mutagen|juggernaut mutagen]], you gain resistance to all physical damage equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 0).
