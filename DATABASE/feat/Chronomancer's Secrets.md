The secrets of time peel back, revealing deeper truths. You gain either the [[DATABASE/spell/Stasis|stasis]] domain spell or the [[DATABASE/spell/Path of Least Resistance|path of least resistance]] focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your pool by 1.
