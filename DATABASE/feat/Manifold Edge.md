# Manifold Edge <span class="item-type">Feat 18</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]]

---
You've learned every possible edge to use against your foes. When you use [[DATABASE/action/Hunt Prey|Hunt Prey]], you can gain a different hunter's edge benefit instead of the one you selected at 1st level. If you do, you don't gain the additional benefit from masterful hunter.
