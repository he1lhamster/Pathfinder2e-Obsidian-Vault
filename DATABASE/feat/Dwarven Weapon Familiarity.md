# Dwarven Weapon Familiarity <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

<span class="item-trait">Dwarf</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]]

---
Your kin have instilled in you an affinity for hard-hitting weapons, and you prefer these to more elegant arms. You are trained with the [[DATABASE/weapon/Battle Axe|battle axe]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Pick|pick]], and [[DATABASE/weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]].
 You also gain access to all uncommon dwarf weapons. For the purpose of determining your proficiency, martial dwarf weapons are simple weapons and advanced dwarf weapons are martial weapons.
