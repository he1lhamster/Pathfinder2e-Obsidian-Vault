# Devil Magic <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Hellspawn|Hellspawn]]

---
Drawing on the infernal power of your sinister forbears, you mislead your foes with magical deception. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Invisibility|invisibility]] and [[DATABASE/spell/Misdirection|misdirection]] each once per day as 2nd-level divine innate spells.

## Traits

**Tiefling:**

A creature with this trait has the tiefling versatile heritage. Tieflings are [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Planar Scion|planar scions]] descended from fiends. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by tieflings.
