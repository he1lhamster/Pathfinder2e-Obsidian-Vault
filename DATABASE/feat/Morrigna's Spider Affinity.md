# Morrigna's Spider Affinity <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Duskwalker</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Ancestry Guide|Ancestry Guide]]

---
Much like [[DATABASE/monster/Morrigna|morrignas]], you feel an affinity for spiders. You can cast [[DATABASE/spell/Animal Messenger|animal messenger]] (spiders only), [[DATABASE/spell/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]] (spiders only), and [[DATABASE/spell/Spider Climb|spider climb]] each once per day as 2nd-level divine innate spells.

## Traits

**Duskwalker:**

A creature with this trait has the duskwalker versatile heritage. Duskwalkers are planar scions infused with the supernatural energy of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Psychopomp|psychopomps]]. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by duskwalkers.
