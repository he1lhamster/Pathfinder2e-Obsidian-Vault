# Command Corpse <span class="item-type">Feat 4</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Archetype</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 181. Zombie Feast|Pathfinder #181: Zombie Feast]]
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Corpse Tender|Corpse Tender]]
**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Corpse Tender Dedication|Corpse Tender Dedication]]; ability to cast [[DATABASE/spell/Harm|harm]]

---
You gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Command Undead|Command Undead]] feat, but you can use it only to transform the effects of _harm_ spells you cast on mindless undead. These spells can control creatures with a level equal to or lower than your level – 1 (instead of your level – 3).
