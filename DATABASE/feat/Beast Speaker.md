# Beast Speaker <span class="item-type">Feat 4</span>

You constantly have the effects of [[DATABASE/spell/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]] as a 2nd-level innate primal spell. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Diplomacy|Diplomacy]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Request|Make a Request]] of animals while this spell is active.
