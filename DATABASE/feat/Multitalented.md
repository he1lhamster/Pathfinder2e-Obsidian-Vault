# Multitalented <span class="item-type">Feat 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Human</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]]

---
You've learned to split your focus between multiple classes with ease. You gain a 2nd-level multiclass dedication feat, even if you normally couldn't take another dedication feat until you take more feats from your current archetype.
 If you're a [[DATABASE/ancestry/Half-Elf|half-elf]], you don't need to meet the feat's ability score prerequisites.

## Traits

**Human:**

A creature with this trait is a member of the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Human|human]] ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
