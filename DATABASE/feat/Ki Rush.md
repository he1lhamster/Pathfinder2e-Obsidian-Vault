# Ki Rush <span class="item-type">Feat 1</span>

You can use ki to move with extraordinary speed and make yourself harder to hit. You gain the [[DATABASE/spell/Ki Rush|ki rush]] ki spell and a focus pool of 1 Focus Point. The rules for ki spells are summarized in the [[DATABASE/class_/Monk|Key Terms section on the monk class page]], and the full rules for focus spells appear here.
