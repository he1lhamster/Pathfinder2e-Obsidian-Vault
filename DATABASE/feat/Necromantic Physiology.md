# Necromantic Physiology <span class="item-type">Feat 5</span>

Your unusual physiology has developed in a way that makes it difficult for parasites and other infestations to prey upon you. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to saves against diseases.

## Traits

**Dhampir:**

A creature with this trait has the dhampir versatile heritage. These humanoids are the mortal offspring of [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Vampire|vampires]] and members of other ancestries.
