You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Climb|Climb]], you gain the [[DATABASE/feat/Combat Climber|Combat Climber]] skill feat, and you reduce the number of free hands required to Climb or [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]] by one.
