# Dwarven Pantheon [LG]

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Gods & Magic|Gods & Magic]] 
The dwarven gods are one large family, with [[DATABASE/deity/Torag|Torag]] as its patriarch. He is joined by his brothers [[DATABASE/deity/Magrim|Magrim]] (the Taskmaster) and [[DATABASE/deity/Angradd|Angradd]] (the Forge Fire), his half-sister [[DATABASE/deity/Dranngvit|Dranngvit]] (the Debt Minder), and his wife [[DATABASE/deity/Folgrit|Folgrit]] (the Watchful Mother). His children are [[DATABASE/deity/Bolka|Bolka]] (the Golden Gift), [[DATABASE/deity/Grundinnar|Grundinnar]] (the Peacemaker), [[DATABASE/deity/Kols|Kols]] (the Oath-Keeper), and [[DATABASE/deity/Trudd|Trudd]] (the Mighty). Torag’s evil former student [[DATABASE/deity/Droskar|Droskar]] (the Dark Smith), though technically part of the dwarven pantheon, is rarely invoked by any save duergars.
**Category** Pantheons
**Edicts** develop skills useful to your community (especially crafting, mining, and trading), honor your ancestors through traditions and rituals
**Anathema** dishonor your family, willingly break a contract or oath, irreparably damage an ancestral relic
**Areas of Concern** ancestry, crafting, dwarves, relationships
