# Final Words<span class="item-type">Hazard 4</span>

<span class="item-trait">Haunt</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Book of the Dead|Book of the Dead]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 22 (trained) to hear the scratch of a quill upon paper
**Description** As a creature touches a hand-written letter, they witness the letter's author scribe it in the past; each pen stroke across the page carves the letter's text into the creature's flesh.

---
**Disable** DC 24 [[DATABASE/skill/Religion|Religion]] (trained) to ward off the haunt or DC 25 [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] (trained) to dispose of the letter with extreme care before the haunt can form
**Carve in Flesh** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (divine, necromancy, visual) **Trigger** A creature reads the letter; **Effect** The haunt deals 2d8+11 slashing damage to the triggering creature as the words are carved into its flesh (DC 21 basic Will save). On a failure, the creature takes 2d8 [[DATABASE/condition/Persistent Damage|persistent bleed damage]].

---
**Reset** 1 minute
