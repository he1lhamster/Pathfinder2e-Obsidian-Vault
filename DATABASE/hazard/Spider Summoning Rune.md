# Spider Summoning Rune<span class="item-type">Hazard 11</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Complex</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 171. Hurricane's Howl|Pathfinder #171: Hurricane's Howl]]
**Complexity** Complex
**Stealth** +24 (master)
**Description** An invisible rune on the floor summons a [[DATABASE/monster/Goliath Spider|goliath spider]] when disturbed by someone who isn't wearing [[DATABASE/deity/Norgorber|Norgorber's]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Religious Symbol|religious symbol]].

---
**Disable** DC 34 [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] (master) to erase the rune or [[DATABASE/spell/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]] (6th level; counteract DC 36) to dispel the rune.
**Summon Animal** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (conjuration, divine, summon) **Trigger** A creature that isn't wearing a [[DATABASE/equipment/Religious Symbol|religious symbol]] of [[DATABASE/deity/Norgorber|Norgorber]] enters the area above the rune; **Effect** The trap summons a [[DATABASE/monster/Goliath Spider|goliath spider]]. The goliath spider rolls initiative and remains for 2d6 rounds, after which the spell ends and the spider disappears. The spider also disappears if someone disables the trap before the duration expires. Unlike most summoned creatures, the goliath spider can use 3 actions each round and can use reactions.

---
**Reset** The trap resets after 24 hours.
