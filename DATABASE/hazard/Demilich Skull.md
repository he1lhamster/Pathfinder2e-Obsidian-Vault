**Description** The skull of a defeated [[Demilich]] is haunted by souls of the demilich's victims.

**Broken Gems** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (arcane) **Trigger** A living creature approaches within 10 feet of the skull; **Effect** The spirits haunting the skull activate one of the skull's ruined gemstones, casting one of the following four spells determined randomly: [[Cast into Time]], [[Chain Lightning]], [[Fireball]] (6th), [[Spellwrack]].
