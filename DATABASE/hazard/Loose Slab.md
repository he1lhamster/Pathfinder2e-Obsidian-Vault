# Loose Slab<span class="item-type">Hazard 3</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Mechanical</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Crown of the Kobold King|Crown of the Kobold King]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 23 (trained)
**Description** The stone slab tears free from its track with the ear-piercing sound of failing metal.

---
**Disable** DC 20 [[DATABASE/skill/Crafting|Crafting]] (expert) to shore up the track so that it functions as intended, or DC 18 [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] (trained) to wedge the slab in place to immobilize it.
**Immunities** critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
**Falling Slab** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A creature attempts to move the slab; **Effect** The slab rips free from its track and falls, slamming down in one direction while it kicks up twisted metal debris in the other. All creatures adjacent to the slab take 3d6 bludgeoning and 3d6 piercing damage (DC 20 basic Reflex save).
