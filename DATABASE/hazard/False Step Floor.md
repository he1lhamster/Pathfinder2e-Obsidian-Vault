**False Step** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (arcane, conjuration, teleportation) **Trigger** A creature enters the room and steps onto the floor; **Effect** The creature slips through the floor; it tumbles through an interdimensional void before falling back into the room through the ceiling and landing on the floor.
