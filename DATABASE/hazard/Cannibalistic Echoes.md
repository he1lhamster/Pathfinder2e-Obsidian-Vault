# Cannibalistic Echoes<span class="item-type">Hazard 16</span>

<span class="item-trait">Haunt</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Book of the Dead|Book of the Dead]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 42 (master) to sense something unusual lurking in the area's echoes
**Description** A pack of cannibalistic spirits swarm through the area, devouring living creatures.

---
**Disable** DC 40 [[DATABASE/skill/Intimidation|Intimidation]] (master) to drive the spirits off with a ferocious display or DC 42 [[DATABASE/skill/Occultism|Occultism]] (expert) to exorcise the spirits
**Feast** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A living creature approaches within 10 feet; **Effect** The spirits bite and chew, dealing 6d12+35 piercing damage to each creature within 30 feet. Affected creatures must each attempt a DC 41 Reflex save.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage.
**Failure** The creature takes full damage, 2d8 [[DATABASE/condition/Persistent Damage|persistent bleed damage]], and is [[DATABASE/condition/Drained|drained 1]].
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage, 4d8 persistent bleed damage, and is drained 2.

---
**Reset** 1 day
