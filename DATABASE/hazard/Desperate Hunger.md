# Desperate Hunger<span class="item-type">Hazard 12</span>

<span class="item-trait">Haunt</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Book of the Dead|Book of the Dead]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 35 (master) to feel an odd sense of hunger
**Description** A skeletally thin spirit appears, weeping as it shoves earth and stones down its throat in an effort to stave off hunger.

---
**Disable** DC 36 [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]] (master) to convince the spirit it's no longer hungry or DC 38 [[DATABASE/skill/Occultism|Occultism]] (trained) to ritually feed the spirit
**Desperate Meal** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A creature approaches within 10 feet; **Effect** Creatures within 30 feet are filled with painful hunger and must succeed at a DC 32 Fortitude save or feel their stomachs fill with rocks, dirt, and worse, dealing 6d10+20 piercing damage.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage and is [[DATABASE/condition/Sickened|sickened 1]].
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and is sickened 2.
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage, is sickened 4, and takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speeds for as long as it's sickened.

---
**Reset** 1 day
