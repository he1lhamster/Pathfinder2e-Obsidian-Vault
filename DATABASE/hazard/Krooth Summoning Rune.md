# Krooth Summoning Rune<span class="item-type">Hazard 8</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Complex</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 152. Legacy of the Lost God|Pathfinder #152: Legacy of the Lost God]]
**Complexity** Complex
**Stealth** +14 (expert)
**Description** The haze conceals a rune that summons a [[DATABASE/monster/Krooth|krooth]] in front of the door to area B10.

---
**Disable** DC 24 [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] to approach without triggering the trap followed by DC 26 [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] (expert) to erase the rune, or [[DATABASE/spell/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]] (4th level; counteract DC 24) to counteract the rune
**Summon Monster** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (arcane, conjuration, summon) **Trigger** A non-[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Xulgath|xulgath]], non-[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Demon|demon]] creature that isn't carrying a [[DATABASE/equipment/Religious Symbol|religious symbol]] of [[DATABASE/deity/Zevgavizeb|Zevgavizeb]] crosses from the north part of the hall to the south part of the hall; **Effect** The trap summons a [[DATABASE/monster/Krooth|krooth]]. The krooth rolls initiative and remains for 2d6 rounds, after which the spell ends and the krooth disappears. The krooth also disappears if someone disables the trap before the duration expires. Unlike most summoned creatures, the krooth can use 3 actions each round and can use reactions.

---
**Reset** The trap resets after 24 hours.
