**Stealth** DC 26 (expert)
**Description** The gold [[DATABASE/equipment/Religious Symbol|religious symbol]] of a former priest of [[DATABASE/deity/Sarenrae|Sarenrae]] is haunted by the priest's tormented soul.

---

**Solar Wrath** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (divine, fire) **Trigger** A non-good creature approaches within 10 feet of the religious symbol; **Effect** The Sarenite summons a burst of spiritual flame in a 30-foot radius. Creatures in the area take 10d8 good damage (DC 26 basic Will save). Creatures who fail this save are also [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 1]], and creatures who critically fail are frightened 3 and [[DATABASE/condition/Fleeing|fleeing]] for 1 round.
