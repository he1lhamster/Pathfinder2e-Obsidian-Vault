# Wronged Monk's Wrath<span class="item-type">Hazard 13</span>

<span class="item-trait">Haunt</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 166. Despair on Danger Island|Pathfinder #166: Despair on Danger Island]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 32 (trained)
**Description** A monk's tormented spirit attacks intruders.

---
**Disable** DC 32 [[DATABASE/deity/Irori|Irori]] [[DATABASE/skill/Lore|Lore]] (trained) to clean the room and return the prayer items to their proper arrangement, DC 37 [[DATABASE/skill/Religion|Religion]] (expert) to state an Iroran prayer of peace and put the haunt to rest, or DC 40 [[DATABASE/skill/Occultism|Occultism]] (master) to create a ward against future hauntings
**Ki Storm** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A creature enters the prayer room; **Effect** The abbot's spirit appears and begins to channel powerful ki energy. One round later, the room fills with a powerful kinetic storm, dealing 3d10+15 electricity damage and 3d10+15 force damage to all creatures in the room. Creatures in the room must attempt a DC 37 Reflex save.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage.
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and becomes [[DATABASE/condition/Deafened|deafened]] for 1 round.
**Critical Failure** The creature takes full damage, 2d8 persistent electricity damage, and becomes deafened for 1 minute.

---
**Reset** The haunt re-forms after 1 hour. The haunt becomes disabled and doesn't re-form if a creature lights all of the candles in the room and offers a prayer to Irori.
