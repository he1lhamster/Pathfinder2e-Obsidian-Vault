# Boulder Deadfall Trap<span class="item-type">Hazard 7</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Mechanical</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 170. Spoken on the Song Wind|Pathfinder #170: Spoken on the Song Wind]]
**Complexity** Simple
**Stealth** DC 25 (trained); DC 0 to spot the balanced boulders
**Description** A weight-sensitive plate buried underneath sand pulls a boulder over a cliff ledge, causing it to fall on targets within the entrance.

---
**Disable** DC 27 [[DATABASE/skill/Crafting|Crafting]] (expert) or DC 25 [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] (expert) to find and disconnect the plate from the tipping mechanism
**Deadfall** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A creature enters the area without taking care to avoid the hidden trigger plate; **Effect** Creatures within 10 feet of the cave entrance take 4d10+18 damage (DC 25 basic Reflex save).

---
**Reset** Resetting the boulders in the trap is a labor-intensive project that takes 2 hours.
