**Stealth** DC 30 (master)
**Description** Enchanted carvings of animals transform any creatures who damage the door into the animal they harmed.

**Animal Form** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A creature damages the door; **Effect** The trap casts [[DATABASE/spell/Bestial Curse|bestial curse]] on the triggering creature (DC 32 Fortitude save); on a critical failure, the duration is 2 hours.
