**Collapse** <span class="action-icon">5</span> **Trigger** A Small or larger creature walks into either 5-foot-square at the westernmost end of the building; **Effect** The triggering creature falls and takes 50 bludgeoning damage, then an additional 10d6 bludgeoning damage as they and the building's falling timbers “land” in a devastating crash at area **A1**.
