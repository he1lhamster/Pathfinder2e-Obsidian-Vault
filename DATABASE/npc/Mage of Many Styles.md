# Mage of Many Styles

The mage of many styles combines magical and martial tactics. They try to hamper foes with [[DATABASE/spell/Black Tentacles|black tentacles]] or [[DATABASE/spell/Confusion|confusion]], switching to damaging spells like [[DATABASE/spell/Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]] once their opponents are properly lined up. When engaged in melee, they make liberal use of [[DATABASE/spell/Shocking Grasp|shocking grasp]] and [[DATABASE/spell/True Strike|true strike]] to deal powerful blows.
