# Strong Oak

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Impossible Lands|Impossible Lands]]
Most ghorans have bodies of pliable vines and tough stems, but you're covered in sturdy, rough bark and your legs are stable as roots. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus on your Fortitude or Reflex DC against attempts to [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grapple]] or [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]] you. This bonus also applies to saving throws against effects that would [[DATABASE/condition/Grabbed|grab]] you, [[DATABASE/condition/Restrained|restrain]] you, or knock you [[DATABASE/condition/Prone|prone]]. You also gain a +2 circumstance bonus on [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Balance|Balance]].
