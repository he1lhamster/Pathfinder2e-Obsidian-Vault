# Cheese Crostata<span class="item-type">Meal 5</span>

**Source** [[Kingmaker Companion Guide]]
**Recipe Price** 8 gp
**Ingredients** 4 basic
**Preparation** DC 22 [[Lore]] or DC 24 [[Survival]]
**Favorite Meal** +1 status bonus to [[Religion]] checks.

---
This pie-like meal is filled with a delightful mix of different cheeses and spices, all cooked into a flaky dough crust.
**Critical Success** The eater gains a +1 status bonus to saving throws against effects that cause the [[Enfeebled]] condition or [[Fatigued]], and during the next 2 days, they can go for up to 20 hours without sleep before becoming fatigued.
**Success** For the next 24 hours, the eater can go for up to 18 hours without sleep before becoming fatigued.
**Critical Failure** The heavy meal doesn't sit well. The eater is [[Sickened]] until they finish a full night's rest.
