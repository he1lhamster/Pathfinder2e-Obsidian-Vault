This traditional Galtan dish consists of a highly seasoned stew of meat and vegetables.
**Critical Success** The eater receives a +1 status bonus to saving throws against effects that cause the [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy]] condition or [[DATABASE/condition/Fatigued|fatigue]], and during the next 2 days, they can go for up to 18 hours without sleep before becoming fatigued.
**Success** During the next 24 hours, the eater can go for up to 18 hours without sleep before becoming fatigued.
