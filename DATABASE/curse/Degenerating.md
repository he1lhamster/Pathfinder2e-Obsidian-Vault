# Degenerating<span class="item-type">Curse 5</span>

<span class="trait-rare item-trait">Rare</span><span class="item-trait">Acid</span><span class="item-trait">Cursed</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Gamemastery Guide|Gamemastery Guide]]
**Usage** curses a weapon
Failure makes the weapon crumble. Whenever you critically fail an attack roll with the weapon, the _degenerating_ curse deals 1d10 acid damage to the weapon, ignoring its Hardness and resistances.
