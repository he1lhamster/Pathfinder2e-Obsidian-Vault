# Blackfrost<span class="item-type">Curse 10</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Cold</span><span class="item-trait">Curse</span><span class="item-trait">Disease</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span><span class="item-trait">Occult</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 189. Dreamers of the Nameless Spires|Pathfinder #189: Dreamers of the Nameless Spires]]
This affliction can't be reduced below stage 1, nor the damage from it healed, until successfully treated with [[DATABASE/spell/Remove Curse|remove curse]] or a similar effect; the affliction can then be removed as a normal disease. A creature that dies from cold damage while cursed with blackfrost rises as a [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Blackfrost Dead|blackfrost dead]] after 24 hours.
**Saving Throw** DC 27 Fortitude; **Effect** **Stage 1** 4d6 cold damage (1 minute); **Stage 2** weakness to cold 5 and [[DATABASE/condition/Drained|drained 1]] (1 day); **Stage 3** weakness to cold 10 and drained 2 (1 day)
