# Frightful Presence

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Bestiary|Bestiary]]
(aura, emotion, fear, mental) A creature that first enters the area must attempt a Will save. Regardless of the result of the saving throw, the creature is temporarily immune to this monster’s Frightful Presence for 1 minute.
 **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected by the presence.
 **Success** The creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 1]].
 **Failure** The creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 2]].
 **Critical Failure** The creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 4]].
