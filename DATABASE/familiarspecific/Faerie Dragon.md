**Frequency** once per hour; **Effect** The faerie dragon breathes euphoric gas in a 10-foot cone. Each creature in the area must attempt a Fortitude save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher. A creature that fails its save is [[DATABASE/condition/Stupefied|stupefied 2]] and [[DATABASE/condition/Slowed|slowed 1]] for 1d4 rounds; on a critical failure, the duration is 1 minute.
