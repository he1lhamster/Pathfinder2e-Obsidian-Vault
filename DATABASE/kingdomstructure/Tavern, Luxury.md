---
**Lots** 2; **Cost** 48 RP, 10 Lumber, 8 Luxuries, 8 Stone
**Construction** [[DATABASE/skill/Trade|Trade]] (master) DC 26
**Upgrade From** [[DATABASE/kingdomstructure/Tavern, Popular|Tavern, Popular]]

**Item Bonus** +2 item bonus to [[DATABASE/action/Hire Adventurers|Hire Adventurers]] and to [[DATABASE/action/Rest and Relax|Rest and Relax]] using Trade
**Effects** The first time you build a luxury tavern in a Kingdom turn, reduce Unrest by 1d4+1. All checks made to [[DATABASE/action/Gather Information|Gather Information]] in a settlement with at least one luxury tavern gain a +2 item bonus.
