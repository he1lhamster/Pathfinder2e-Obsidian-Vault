This stout staff is made from gnarled hawthorn. When used as a weapon, a _staff of nature's vengeance_ permanently has the effects of [[Shillelagh]].
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells, plus one casting of _shillelagh_.
