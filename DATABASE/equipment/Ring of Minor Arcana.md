# Ring of Minor Arcana<span class="item-type">Item 5</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Arcane</span><span class="item-trait">Evocation</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/The Fall of Plaguestone|The Fall of Plaguestone]]
**Price** 160 gp
**Usage** worn; **Bulk** —

---
This rose-gold ring is adorned in the center by a somewhat ominous-looking horned skull. This ring gives you the power to cast the following innate arcane cantrips any number of times each day: [[DATABASE/spell/Detect Magic|detect magic]], [[DATABASE/spell/Mage Hand|mage hand]], and [[DATABASE/spell/Prestidigitation|prestidigitation]]. Each is cast as a 1st-level spell. If you are an arcane spellcaster, these can instead be heightened to the level of your cantrips.
