This brown-and-green tunic is embroidered with patterns that resemble interlocking elk antlers. You gain a +2 item bonus to Nature checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You gain 1 Focus Point, which you can spend only to cast an order spell. If you don’t spend this Focus Point by the end of this turn, it is lost.
**Craft Requirements** You are a druid.
