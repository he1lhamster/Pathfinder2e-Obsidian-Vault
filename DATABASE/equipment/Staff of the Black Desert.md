This rough metal staff is pitted and sandblasted, with thousands of nigh-imperceptible sand crystals embedded in its surface. While carrying the staff, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Occultism|Occultism]] checks to identify aberrations and oozes native to the Darklands.
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
