# Malleable Mixture<span class="item-type">Item 8+</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Alchemical</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Elixir</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Grand Bazaar|Grand Bazaar]]
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact

---
Your bones, muscles, and organs become vastly softer and more pliable. You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were smaller than your normal size, and you gain an item bonus to your Fortitude and Reflex DCs against attempts to [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grapple]], [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shove]], or [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]] you.

## Malleable Mixture (Lesser)<span class="item-type">Item 8</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Grand Bazaar|Grand Bazaar]]
**Price** 75 gp
**Bulk** L
You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were 1 size smaller than your normal size (minimum Small). The bonus to your DCs is +2, and the duration is 1 minute.

## Malleable Mixture (Greater)<span class="item-type">Item 14</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Grand Bazaar|Grand Bazaar]]
**Price** 750 gp
**Bulk** L
You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were 2 sizes smaller than your normal size (minimum Tiny). The bonus to your DCs is +3, and the duration is 10 minutes.
