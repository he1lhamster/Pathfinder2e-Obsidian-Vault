Resembling two hands clasped in friendship, this _[[Diplomat's Badge]]_ is designed to be woven into the wearer's braided beard. When the wearer befriends others, one of the hands briefly transforms to resemble the befriended creature's own.
**Forming the Bond** The PCs complete the [[Rite of Repatriation]].**Advancing the Bond** Solve problems nonviolently. End ancient grudges and heal old wounds, especially those caused by dwarves. 
**Minor Gifts** [[Ancestors' Call]], [[Linguistics Nexus]], [[Repository of Knowledge]] (Dwarf Lore, Genealogy Lore, Warfare Lore)
 **Major Gifts** [[Perception Filter]], or increase the diplomat's badge's item bonus to Diplomacy to +2.
