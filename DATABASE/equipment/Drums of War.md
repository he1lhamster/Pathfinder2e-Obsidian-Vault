This handheld snare drum is adorned with garish scenes of battle and triumph. When played, no matter what rhythm, it always gives the impression of a marching beat, invoking armies on the move. While playing the drums, you gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]] checks and a +5-foot status bonus to your Speed.
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from this instrument to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
