# Egg Cream Fizz<span class="item-type">Item 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Alchemical</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Elixir</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Price** 27 gp
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact

---
Containing neither eggs nor cream, an egg cream fizz contains milk or nut milk, sparkling water, and flavored syrup, frothed and chilled. Upon drinking, you feel lighter and more buoyant, gaining a +5-foot item bonus to your Speed for 10 minutes. During this time, you also gain another effect determined by the drink's syrup, which is chosen when the drink is created.

* **Chocolate** You gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Balance|Balance]], [[DATABASE/action/Maneuver in Flight|Maneuver in Flight]], and [[DATABASE/action/Squeeze|Squeeze]].
* **Strawberry** You gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Climb|Climb]], [[DATABASE/action/Leap|Leap]], and [[DATABASE/action/Swim|Swim]].
* **Vanilla** You can Step into difficult terrain.
