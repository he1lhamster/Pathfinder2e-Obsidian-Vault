Hunters all over Golarion favor curare, a potent paralytic derived from boiled tree bark.
 **Saving Throw** DC 25 Fortitude; **Maximum Duration** 6 rounds (but see stage 3); **Stage 1** 2d6 poison damage, [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy 1]], and [[DATABASE/condition/Enfeebled|enfeebled 1]] (1 round); **Stage 2** 3d6 poison damage, clumsy 2, enfeebled 2, and [[DATABASE/condition/Slowed|slowed 1]] (1 minute); **Stage 3** 4d6 poison damage, clumsy 2, enfeebled 2, and slowed 1 (1 round). If the victim fails the saving throw while at Stage 3, the poison ends and the victim is [[DATABASE/condition/Paralyzed|paralyzed]] for 2d6 minutes.
