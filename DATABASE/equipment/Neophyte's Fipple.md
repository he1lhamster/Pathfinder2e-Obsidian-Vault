**Usage** held in 2 hands; **Bulk** L
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> [[DATABASE/action/Perform|Perform]] **Requirements** You are untrained in [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]].

---
Made of polished wood, a _neophyte's fipple_ is a block flute enchanted to guarantee melodic sound. When you Perform a song on the fipple to Activate it, your ability modifier, proficiency bonus, and item bonus for the Performance check total +7, regardless of what they would normally be. Add other bonuses and penalties to the check normally. Once the magic is used, the fipple remains as a mundane [[DATABASE/equipment/Musical Instrument|instrument]].
