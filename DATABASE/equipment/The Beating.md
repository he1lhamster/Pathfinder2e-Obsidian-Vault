**Suit** Hammers (Str); **Alignment** neutral evil

---
_The Beating_ symbolizes attack from all sides. If you have _The Beating_ invested, whenever you and an ally are flanking a foe, your melee Strikes gain a +2 item bonus to damage. This increases to a +3 item bonus at 17th level.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You wave the card to conjure up dozens of violent ghostly figures; you cast [[Pernicious Poltergeist]], and the area of the spell is treated as difficult terrain in addition to its normal effects. The level of the spell is one-half your level, rounded down (minimum 6th level), and the save DC is your class DC.
