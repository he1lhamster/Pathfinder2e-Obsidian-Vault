Thousands of small, reflective, mirrored glass shards have been carefully stitched down this long silk duster.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Requirements** The _mirror robe_ was last activated to draw attention toward you, or you haven't used the _mirror robe_ today; **Effect** The _mirror robe_ [[DATABASE/action/Create a Diversion|Creates a Diversion]] for you with a +9 [[DATABASE/skill/Deception|Deception]] modifier.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact (visual) **Requirements** The _mirror robe_ was last activated to divert attention away from you and you are [[DATABASE/condition/Hidden|hidden]] or [[DATABASE/condition/Undetected|undetected]] by at least one foe; **Effect** You draw attention toward yourself. Choose one foe to which you were hidden or undetected. You reveal yourself to all, becoming [[DATABASE/condition/Observed|observed]]. The foe you chose diverts its attention to you, becoming [[DATABASE/condition/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]] to your allies until the beginning of your next turn. If you are [[DATABASE/condition/Invisible|invisible]] or otherwise can't become observed, you can't use this activation.
