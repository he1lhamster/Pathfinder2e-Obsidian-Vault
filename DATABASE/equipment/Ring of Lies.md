This plain silver ring has an almost oily sheen. While wearing the ring, you gain a +2 item bonus to Deception checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** Snapping your fingers on the hand that wears the ring causes the ring to cast [[DATABASE/spell/Glibness|glibness]] on you with no visual manifestations of a spell being cast.
