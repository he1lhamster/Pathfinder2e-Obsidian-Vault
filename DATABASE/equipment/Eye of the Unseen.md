This prosthetic eye was designed by elven crafters but comes in a range of appearances for different ancestries. While wearing the eye, you gain a +1 item bonus to visual Perception checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You focus on the eye to see the unseen. The eye casts [[DATABASE/spell/See Invisibility|see invisibility]] on you.
