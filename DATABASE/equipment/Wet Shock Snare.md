# Wet Shock Snare<span class="item-type">Item 5</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Electricity</span><span class="item-trait">Mechanical</span><span class="item-trait">Snare</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>

**Price** 25 gp
**Bulk** —

---
A hidden copper plate conceals a bag of electric eels. When a creature steps on the plate, the eels become agitated, and the creature takes 4d8 electricity damage (DC 21 basic Reflex save). On a critical failure, the current causes the creature to become [[DATABASE/condition/Stunned|stunned 2]]. The eels are unharmed after use, but the mechanism is broken as usual for snares.
