This gnarled staff is covered with thorns and coated with a glistening lacquer that acts as a minor irritant. While holding the staff, you aren't affected by its coating or thorns, and you reduce the DC of your flat checks to overcome [[DATABASE/condition/Persistent Damage|persistent poison damage]].
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
