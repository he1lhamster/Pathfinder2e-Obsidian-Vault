# Black Tendril Shot<span class="item-type">Item 7+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Conjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Ammunition** any; **Bulk** —
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> command

---
A glistening, tar-like substance that's displeasing to the touch coats a _black tendril shot_. When the activated ammunition hits a target, it exudes tendrils that encase the target, which must attempt a Fortitude saving throw. The ammunition's effects last until the target [[DATABASE/action/Escape|Escapes]]. DCs for the saving throw and Escape vary by type.
**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.
**Success** The target is [[DATABASE/condition/Slowed|slowed 1]] but gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Escape the ammunition's effect.
**Failure** The target is slowed 1.
**Critical Failure** The target is slowed 2.
