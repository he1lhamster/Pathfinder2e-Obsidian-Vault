This _+1 [[DATABASE/equipment/Resilient|resilient]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Energy-Resistant|fire-resistant]] [[DATABASE/armor/Niyaháat|niyaháat]]_ is often created to mark the passage of an Erutaki warrior into adulthood, formed from the chitinous plates of a remorhaz, with larger pieces protecting the chest, shoulders, and head. **Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> envision **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** You're dealt 30 or more fire damage, before any reduction from your fire resistance; **Requirements** You've used the _fire shield_ activation already today; **Effect** The _fire shield_ activation recharges.
