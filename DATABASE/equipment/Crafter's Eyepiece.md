# Crafter's Eyepiece<span class="item-type">Item 3+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Usage** worn eyepiece; **Bulk** —

---
This rugged metal eyepiece etched with square patterns is designed to be worn over a single eye. Twisting the lens reveals a faint three-dimensional outline of an item you plan to build or repair, with helpful labels on the component parts. You gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Crafting|Crafting]] checks. When you [[DATABASE/action/Repair|Repair]] an item, increase the Hit Points restored to 10 + 10 per proficiency rank on a success or 15 + 15 per proficiency rank on a critical success.
