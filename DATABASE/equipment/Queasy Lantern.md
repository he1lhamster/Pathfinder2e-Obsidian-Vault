# Queasy Lantern<span class="item-type">Item 7+</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Light</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span>
**Source** [[Grand Bazaar]]
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** 1

---
This bull's-eye lantern is wrapped in decrepit leather skin. It constantly emits light in a 60-foot cone (and dim light in the next 60 feet). You can close or open internal shutters with an Interact action to block or reveal the light.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You slide in a magical lens that causes the lantern to emit a pale green light and then aim the lantern. All creatures in the lantern's 60-foot cone of bright light (but not those in the dim light), must attempt a DC 23 Fortitude save. On a failure, a creature becomes [[Sickened]] (sickened 2 on a critical failure). The light then reverts to normal as the lens slides out of place.
