# Heedless Spurs<span class="item-type">Item 15</span>

<span class="trait-rare item-trait">Rare</span><span class="item-trait">Abjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 155. Lord of the Black Sands|Pathfinder #155: Lord of the Black Sands]]
**Price** 5,200 gp
**Usage** worn anklets; **Bulk** L

---
These wicked-looking spiked metal wheels fit around the ankles and jingle when the wearer walks, imposing a –1 item penalty on [[DATABASE/skill/Stealth|Stealth]] checks. If used as a weapon, they are treated as a [[DATABASE/weapon/Spiked Gauntlet|spiked gauntlet]].
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Frequency** once per 10 minutes; **Requirements** You are riding a mount; **Effect** You kick both spurs into your mount's flank. Your mount takes 2d6 [[DATABASE/condition/Persistent Damage|persistent bleed damage]] and gains the [[DATABASE/condition/Quickened|quickened]] condition for 1 minute or until the persistent bleed damage ends, whichever comes first. It can use the extra action only to Stride.
