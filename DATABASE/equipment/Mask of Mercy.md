This porcelain or alabaster mask portrays an angelic visage of kindness and mercy. The mask grants a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Medicine|Medicine]] checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> envision (fortune) **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** You are about to roll a variable number of Hit Points you restore from an action with the healing trait; **Effect** Roll twice to determine the number of Hit Points you restore and take the higher result.
