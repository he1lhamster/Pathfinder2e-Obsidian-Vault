This flexible _+2 striking [[DATABASE/weapon/Sap|sap]]_ is crafted from the stalks of cave fungi and bound around a mesh of spore-infused gills. Demon-worshipping [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Xulgath|xulgaths]] use _spore saps_ to capture spellcasters alive for later sacrifice.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> Interact (poison) **Frequency** three times per day; **Trigger** You successfully Strike a creature with the _spore sap_; **Effect** The _spore sap_ releases a puff of stupefying fungal spores. The target must succeed at a DC 28 Fortitude save or become [[DATABASE/condition/Stupefied|stupefied 1]] for 1 minute (stupefied 2 on a critical failure).
