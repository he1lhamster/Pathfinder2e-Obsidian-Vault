# Spore Sap<span class="item-type">Item 11</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Evocation</span><span class="item-trait">Fungus</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[Pathfinder 154. Siege of the Dinosaurs]]
**Price** 1,250 gp
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L
**Base Weapon** [[Sap]]

---
This flexible _+2 striking [[Sap]]_ is crafted from the stalks of cave fungi and bound around a mesh of spore-infused gills. Demon-worshipping [[Xulgath]] use _spore saps_ to capture spellcasters alive for later sacrifice.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> Interact (poison) **Frequency** three times per day; **Trigger** You successfully Strike a creature with the _spore sap_; **Effect** The _spore sap_ releases a puff of stupefying fungal spores. The target must succeed at a DC 28 Fortitude save or become [[Stupefied]] for 1 minute (stupefied 2 on a critical failure).
