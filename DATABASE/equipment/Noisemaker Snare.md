# Noisemaker Snare<span class="item-type">Item 2</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Clockwork</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Fire</span><span class="item-trait">Mechanical</span><span class="item-trait">Snare</span><span class="item-trait">Trap</span>
**Source** [[Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 6 gp
**Bulk** —
**Access** Characters from Absalom, New Thassilon, Alkenstar, Dongun Hold, or especially technological regions of Arcadia like the Deadshot Lands have access to uncommon clockwork items from the first two chapters of this book.

---
When a creature enters this snare's square, it triggers an extremely loud clockwork device, which explodes with a bang that can be heard from 200 feet away and deals 1d8 fire damage. The creature must attempt a DC 18 Reflex save.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage.
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and is [[Deafened]] for 1 round.
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and is deafened for 1 minute.
