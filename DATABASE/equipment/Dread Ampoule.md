This flask is filled with a murky purple gas that briefly interferes with normal brain activity. A dread ampoule deals the listed mental damage and mental splash damage. On a hit, the target becomes [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 1]], or frightened 2 on a critical hit. Many types also grant an item bonus to attack rolls.
