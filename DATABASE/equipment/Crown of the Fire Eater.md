A wreath of flames dances around the rim of this golden crown. You gain resistance 5 to fire.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> comman **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** You take fire damage; **Effect** You absorb some of the flame that would harm you. Increase your fire resistance from the crown from 5 to 15. Just after taking any remaining fire damage, you regain a number of Hit Points equal to 15 or the fire damage dealt by the triggering attack before damage resistance, whichever is less.
