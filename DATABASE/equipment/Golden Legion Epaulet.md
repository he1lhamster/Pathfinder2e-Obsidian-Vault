These gleaming golden epaulets are issued to Eagle Knight officers and worn as part of the uniform. While they can be enchanted to provide a variety of effects, the Golden Legion epaulet remains the most common.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> (Command) **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** Issue an order as part of the command. You and each ally within 60 feet who follows that order gain a +1 status bonus to attack rolls, damage rolls, and saving throws against fear until the beginning of your next turn.
