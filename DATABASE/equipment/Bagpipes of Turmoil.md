While those who appreciate bagpipes may like the sound of this gray reed and black leather instrument, its real purpose is to sow turmoil against a performer's enemies, spreading discord with each note. While playing the bagpipes, you gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Performance|Performance]] checks and to [[DATABASE/skill/Intimidation|Intimidation]] checks made to [[DATABASE/action/Demoralize|Demoralize]].
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from this instrument to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
