# Armbands of Athleticism<span class="item-type">Item 9+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Usage** worn armbands; **Bulk** L

---
Skilled awl work has imprinted images of a muscled weightlifter into these tiered leather bands, which grant you enhanced stamina and skill when performing athletic exercises. While fastened to your upper arms, the armbands give you a +2 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks. In addition, whenever you use an action to Climb or Swim and you succeed at the Athletics check, add a +5-foot item bonus to the distance you move.
