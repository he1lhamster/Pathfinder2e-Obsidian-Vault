The tip of a metallic dragon's claw is set into a band of silver. When you summon dragons using the claw, you must choose [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Metallic|metallic dragons]].

* **Armor** After you cast a [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Rat|conjuration]] spell by activating the _wyrm claw_, you gain resistance 5 to acid, cold, electricity, and fire for the next minute.
* **Weapon** After you cast a conjuration spell by activating the claw, you gain a Breath Weapon activity that takes 2 actions and deals 6d6 damage with a DC 28 basic Reflex save. Choose the area and type of damage when you use Breath Weapon: a 30-foot line that deals acid, electricity, or fire, or a 15-foot cone that deals cold or fire. You can use this activity once before the end of your next turn.
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You cast [[DATABASE/spell/Gouging Claw|gouging claw]].
