# Wyrm Claw<span class="item-type">Item 12+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Conjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Spellheart</span>
**Source** [[Treasure Vault]] 
**Usage** affixed to armor or a weapon; **Bulk** —

---
The tip of a metallic dragon's claw is set into a band of silver. When you summon dragons using the claw, you must choose [[Dragon, Metallic]].

* **Armor** After you cast a [[Rat]] spell by activating the _wyrm claw_, you gain resistance 5 to acid, cold, electricity, and fire for the next minute.
* **Weapon** After you cast a conjuration spell by activating the claw, you gain a Breath Weapon activity that takes 2 actions and deals 6d6 damage with a DC 28 basic Reflex save. Choose the area and type of damage when you use Breath Weapon: a 30-foot line that deals acid, electricity, or fire, or a 15-foot cone that deals cold or fire. You can use this activity once before the end of your next turn.
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You cast [[Gouging Claw]].
