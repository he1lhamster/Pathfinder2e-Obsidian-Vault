_The Tangled Briar_ signifies the return of ancient triumphs. As long as you have _The Tangled Briar_ invested, you gain [[DATABASE/feat/Toughness|Toughness]] as a bonus feat. If you already possess this feat, increase your maximum Hit Points by twice your level.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You raise the card up above your head and invoke the thorns and briars of the tangled parts of the world; you cast [[DATABASE/spell/Wall of Thorns|wall of thorns]]. The level of the spell is half your level, rounded down (minimum 6th level). The brambles within this particular wall of thorns are treated as greater difficult terrain.
