# Dust of Disappearance<span class="item-type">Item 9</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Illusion</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Price** 135 gp
**Bulk** —
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact

---
This powder shimmers like a thousand tiny motes of light. Activating the dust by sprinkling it on yourself or a creature within reach casts a 4th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Invisibility|invisibility]] spell with a duration of 1 minute on that creature. This invisibility can’t be negated or seen through by any spell of 3rd level or lower or any item of 5th level or lower.
