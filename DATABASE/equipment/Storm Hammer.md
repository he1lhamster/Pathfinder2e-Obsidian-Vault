Sparks of crackling electricity arc from this _+1 magic [[Warhammer]]_, and the head thrums with distant thunder. Any hit with this hammer deals 1 extra electricity damage. You can use a special action while holding the hammer to transform the sparks into lightning bolts.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> concentrate ; **Effect** Until the end of your turn, the hammer deals 1d6 extra electricity damage instead of just 1. After you use this action, you can't use it again for 10 minutes.
