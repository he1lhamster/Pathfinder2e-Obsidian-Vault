Your limbs become extremely limber, letting you stretch and twist to extreme degrees at the cost of fine motor skills. 
**Benefit** You gain the listed item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Escape|Escape]], [[DATABASE/action/Squeeze|Squeeze]], and [[DATABASE/action/Tumble Through|Tumble Through]], and you increase your reach by the listed amount. 
**Drawback** You take a –1 penalty to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks, [[DATABASE/skill/Stealth|Stealth]] checks, [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] checks, and attack rolls, and a –1 penalty per damage die to all weapon and unarmed attack damage.
