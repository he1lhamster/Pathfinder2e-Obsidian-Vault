This _+2 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|greater striking]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Shock|shock]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ has a broad, flat blade that can support the weight of a Medium-sized or smaller wielder. Magic allows the weapon to soar through the air, carrying its wielder along with it.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> command **Frequency** once per day; **Requirements** You're holding the sword in at least one hand; **Effect** You leap upon your sword as it soars through the air for up to 10 minutes. You gain a fly Speed of 25 feet or your land Speed, whichever is slower, but you can't Strike with your weapon except via the weapon's other activation (see below). You can Dismiss the activation, and it ends automatically if you cease holding the weapon with at least one hand.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact **Requirements** You are riding on your weapon; **Effect** You ride your weapon into a foe, attacking it as you fly past. You Fly, making a melee Strike with the weapon once at any point during your movement.
