**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> envision (metamagic) **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** If your next action is to cast a conjuration spell prepared from this spellbook that summons one or more creatures, you infuse one of the summoned creatures with the spell's energies, causing it to arrive with 10 temporary Hit Points that last for up to 1 minute.
