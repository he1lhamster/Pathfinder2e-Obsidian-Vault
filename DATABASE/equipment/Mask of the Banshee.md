# Mask of the Banshee<span class="item-type">Item 9+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Auditory</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Necromancy</span><span class="item-trait">Negative</span>

**Usage** worn mask; **Bulk** —

---
This ice-blue half-mask is adorned with a wicked silver grin that covers the wearer's mouth, leaving the rest of the face uncovered. You gain a +2 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Intimidation|Intimidation]] checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** The mask emits a soul-chilling scream that deals 6d10 negative damage to each living creature in a 20-foot emanation (DC 25 basic Fortitude save).
**Craft Requirements** Supply a casting of [[DATABASE/spell/Wail of the Banshee|wail of the banshee]].
