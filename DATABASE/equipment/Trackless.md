_Trackless_ runes are common among hunters and trappers, as well as thieves and anyone fleeing pursuit. While wearing _trackless_ footwear, you have a +4 item bonus to the DC to track you. However, this bonus doesn't stack with the status bonus from [[DATABASE/spell/Pass Without Trace|pass without trace]].
