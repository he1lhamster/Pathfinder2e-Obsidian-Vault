A two-pronged metal rod, this wand has a handle coated in thick rubber. Each prong ends in a copper coil. When Activated, the wand produces a loud zapping sound.
**Activate** Cast a Spell **Frequency** once per day, plus overcharge; **Effect** You cast [[Paralyze]], electrocuting the target into immobility. Each target takes electricity damage at the start of its turns while it remains [[Stunned]] or [[Paralyzed]] due to the spell. The amount of damage depends on the wand's type.
**Craft Requirements** Supply a casting of _paralyze_ of the appropriate level.
