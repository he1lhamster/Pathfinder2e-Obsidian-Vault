# Goggles of Night<span class="item-type">Item 5+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Usage** worn eyepiece; **Bulk** —

---
The opaque crystal lenses of these sleek goggles do not obscure vision, but rather enhance it. While wearing the goggles, you gain a +1 item bonus to Perception checks involving sight.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** Rotating the lenses 90 degrees, you gain darkvision for 1 hour.
