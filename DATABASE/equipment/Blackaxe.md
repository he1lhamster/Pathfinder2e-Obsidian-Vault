# Blackaxe<span class="item-type">Item 25</span>

<span class="trait-unique item-trait">Unique</span><span class="item-trait">Artifact</span><span class="item-trait">Cursed</span><span class="item-trait">Evocation</span><span class="item-trait">Primal</span>

**Usage** held in 2 hands; **Bulk** 2
**Base Weapon** [[Greataxe]]

---
This potent weapon used by [[Treerazer]] is a _+4 [[Corrosive]] [[Striking]] obsidian [[Greataxe]]_ that grants a +4 item bonus to attack rolls, deals an extra 2d6 damage to plants, and has the properties of [[Adamantine Weapon]].
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> (divination, scrying) **Requirements** You aren't wielding _Blackaxe_ and you are its true owner. ; **Effect** You sense the world around _Blackaxe_ as though you were in its location and can use any of your innate spells through the link as if it were the source of the spell. If another creature is wielding _Blackaxe_, it must succeed at a DC 50 Will save or be [[Slowed]] until it relinquishes the weapon.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> command (conjuration, teleportation) **Requirements** You aren't wielding _Blackaxe_ and you are its true owner.; **Effect** _Blackaxe_ appears in your hands, teleporting instantly from its prior location.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> command (death, healing, necromancy) **Frequency** once per minute; **Effect** Make a Strike against a living tree with _Blackaxe_. If it hits, the tree withers to ash and you heal 250 Hit Points and gain the benefit of [[Restoration]] (6th) and [[Restore Senses]].
