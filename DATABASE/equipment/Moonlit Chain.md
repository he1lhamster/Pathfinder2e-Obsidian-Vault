# Moonlit Chain<span class="item-type">Item 7</span>

<span class="item-trait">Divination</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[Core Rulebook]] 
**Price** 360 gp
**Usage** worn armor; **Bulk** 2
**Base Armor** [[Chain Shirt]]

---
This _+1 [[Silver Armor]] [[Chain Shirt]]_ has a collar adorned with stitched images of the phases of the moon. You can see in moonlight as though you had low-light vision.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Frequency** one per day; **Effect** You touch the stitched image of the new moon on the armor’s collar and suppress the [[Dazzled]] condition for 1 minute.
**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include 33 gp of silver.
