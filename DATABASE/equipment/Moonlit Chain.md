This _+1 [[DATABASE/equipment/Silver Armor|silver]] [[DATABASE/armor/Chain Shirt|chain shirt]]_ has a collar adorned with stitched images of the phases of the moon. You can see in moonlight as though you had low-light vision.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Frequency** one per day; **Effect** You touch the stitched image of the new moon on the armor’s collar and suppress the [[DATABASE/condition/Dazzled|dazzled]] condition for 1 minute.
**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include 33 gp of silver.
