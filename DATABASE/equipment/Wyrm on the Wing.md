# Wyrm on the Wing<span class="item-type">Item 9+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Abjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Tattoo</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Usage** tattooed on the body; **Bulk** —

---
Depicting a majestic dragon in flight, this tattoo imparts the endurance and majesty of those powerful creatures. You gain resistance 5 to the damage type matching the dragon your tattoo depicts.

* **[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Black|Black]] or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Copper|Copper]]** Acid
* **[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Blue|Blue]] or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Bronze|Bronze]]** Electricity
* **[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Brass|Brass]], [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Gold|Gold]], or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Red|Red]]** Fire
* **[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Green|Green]]** Poison
* **[[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Silver|Silver]] or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, White|White]]** Cold
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** The tattoo casts [[DATABASE/spell/Dragon Wings|dragon wings]] on you. You can add the [[DATABASE/spell/Dragon Claws|dragon claws]] benefit only if you know that focus spell and spend 1 Focus Point when you Activate the tattoo.
