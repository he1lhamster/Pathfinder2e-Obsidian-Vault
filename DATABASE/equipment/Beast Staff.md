# Beast Staff<span class="item-type">Item 7+</span>

<span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Staff</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** 1

---
The visages of beasts are carved into the painted wood of a beast staff, with a large head on top. When used as a weapon, the staff is a _+1 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|striking]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Staff|staff]]_. While wielding the staff while you have it prepared, you're affected by [[DATABASE/spell/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]. If you have [[DATABASE/class_/Druid|Wild Empathy]], you gain a +1 circumstance bonus on checks using it. 
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
