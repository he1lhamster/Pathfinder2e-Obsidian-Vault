# Concealed Holster<span class="item-type">Item 3</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 25 gp
**Bulk** L

---
This leather holster is crafted to better hide small firearms from view. Only firearms designed for use in one hand are small enough to disguise with this holster. You gain a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Stealth|Stealth]] checks and DCs to hide or conceal a firearm or hand crossbow in this holster.
