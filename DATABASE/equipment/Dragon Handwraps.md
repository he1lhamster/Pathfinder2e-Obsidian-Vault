# Dragon Handwraps<span class="item-type">Item 20</span>

<span class="item-trait">Apex</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Price** 80,000 gp
**Usage** worn gloves; **Bulk** —

---
These silken handwraps feature intricate embroidery of a serpentine red dragon adorned with golden thread. The handwraps function as _+3 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|major striking]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Flaming|greater flaming]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Handwraps of Mighty Blows|handwraps of mighty blows]]_. You also gain a +4 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks made to [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grapple]] or [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shove]]. When you invest the handwraps, you either increase your Strength score by 2 or increase it to 18, whichever is higher.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You slap the bottom of your palms with hands splayed outward, casting a 7th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Burning Hands|burning hands]] spell (DC 41).
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> envision **Frequency** once per hour; **Trigger** You succeed or critically succeed with a Grapple; **Effect** You gain a +2 status bonus to your Athletics DC against any checks made to [[DATABASE/action/Escape|Escape]] your grapple until the end of your next turn.
