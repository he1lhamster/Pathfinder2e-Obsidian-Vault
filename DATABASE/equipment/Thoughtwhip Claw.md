[[DATABASE/deity/Abraxas|Abraxas]] teaches that minds can be robbed as surely as pockets. This tattoo of a clenched fist provides a +2 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Thievery|Thievery]] checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** Abraxas reaches through your hands and creates threads to yank thoughts from the mind of another. The _thoughtwhip claw_ casts [[DATABASE/spell/Mind Probe|mind probe]] on a creature within range, with a DC of 28.
