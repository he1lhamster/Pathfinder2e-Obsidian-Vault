The book's cover is a patchwork of tanned humanoid skin that reeks of rotting meat. The longer a living creature studies this book, the more the scent of decay appeals to them.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> (concentrate, necromancy) **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** Your last action was to cast a necromancy spell prepared from this grimoire; **Effect** You draw negative energy from the triggering spell. You gain 10 temporary hit points that last for 1 hour.
