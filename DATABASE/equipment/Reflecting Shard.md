# Reflecting Shard<span class="item-type">Item 13+</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Abjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Talisman</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Usage** affixed to a shield; **Bulk** —
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> command **Trigger** You are targeted by a spell of 5th level or lower; **Requirements** You're a master in [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]], and you have the affixed shield [[DATABASE/action/Raise a Shield|raised]].

---
This mirrored metal fragment is bolted or welded to the face of the affixed shield. When you Activate it, you attempt to reflect the triggering spell back at its caster with [[DATABASE/spell/Spell Turning|spell turning]], using your Athletics modifier for the counteract check. The talisman's counteract level is 7th.
