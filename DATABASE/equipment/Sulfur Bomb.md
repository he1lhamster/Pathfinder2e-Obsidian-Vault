A thick, sulfurous, irritating gas fills this golden-yellow flask. A sulfur bomb deals the listed damage. On a hit, the target takes a –1 status penalty to Perception checks and attack rolls until the end of its next turn, or becomes [[DATABASE/condition/Sickened|sickened 1]] on a critical hit. Creatures hit with this bomb are temporarily immune to the effects of the bomb for 1 minute.
