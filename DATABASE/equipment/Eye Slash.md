<span class="item-trait">Divination</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Tattoo</span>
**Source** [[Treasure Vault]] 
**Usage** tattooed on the body; **Bulk** —

---
Small scars or marks around your eye improve your distant vision. These scars are especially common among [[Orc]] scouts, who favor scars shaped like eagle talons. You can see four times farther than normal. If you have darkvision, you can see blood in color. Higher-level versions of an _eye slash_ are larger and more elaborate scars or marks, radiating out around the eye.
