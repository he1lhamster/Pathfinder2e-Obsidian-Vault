This suit of _+2 greater resilient [[DATABASE/equipment/Dragonhide Armor|dragonhide]] [[DATABASE/armor/Full Plate|full plate]]_ makes you look like a fearsome dragon. The armor comes in 10 varieties corresponding to the 10 common dragon types, though other varieties undoubtedly exist.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You unleash a bout of dragon breath with a shape, damage type, and saving throw corresponding to the type of dragon used to make the armor (shown on the table below). The breath weapon deals 14d6 damage; each creature in the area must attempt a DC 36 basic saving throw.| 0 | 1 | 2 |
|:----------------|:-----------------|:-------------------------------------|
| Dragon Type | Protects Against | Breath Weapon (Save) |
| Black or copper | acid | 30-foot line of acid (Reflex) |
| Blue or bronze | electricty | 30-foot line of electricity (Reflex) |
| Brass | fire | 30-foot line of fire (Reflex) |
| Green | poison | 15-foot cone of poison (Fortitude) |
| Gold or red | fire | 15-foot cone of fire (Reflex) |
| Silver or white | cold | 15-foot cone of Cold (Reflex) |
**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include 1,250 gp of dragonhide.
