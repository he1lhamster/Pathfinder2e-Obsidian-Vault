# Petrification Cannon<span class="item-type">Item 15</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 6,500 gp
**Usage** held in 2 hands; **Bulk** 2

---
Built from the taxidermic body of a [[DATABASE/monster/Basilisk|basilisk]], a petrification cannon functions as a _+2 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|greater striking]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Double-barreled Musket|double-barreled musket]]_. A _petrification cannon_ can be activated to fire a beam of energy that transforms flesh into stone.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> Interact (magical, transmutation) **Frequency** once per hour; **Effect** A beam of coiling energy leaps from the _petrification cannon_ at a target within 60 feet. The target must attempt a Fortitude save against DC 34 with the effects of [[DATABASE/spell/Flesh to Stone|flesh to stone]].
**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include the body of a basilisk.
