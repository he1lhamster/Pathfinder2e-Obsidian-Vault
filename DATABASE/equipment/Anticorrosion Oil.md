# Anticorrosion Oil<span class="item-type">Item 2</span>

<span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Oil</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Price** 6 gp
**Usage** held in 2 hands; **Bulk** L
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact

---
You can coat objects of 6 Bulk or less with _anticorrosion oil_. For 24 hours, the object takes half damage from acid and from all effects that specifically cause it to rust or corrode, such as contact with a [[DATABASE/monster/Rust Monster|rust monster's]] antennae.
