# Sandals of the Stag<span class="item-type">Item 18</span>

<span class="item-trait">Apex</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Treasure Vault|Treasure Vault]] 
**Price** 24,000 gp
**Usage** worn sandals; **Bulk** L

---
These sturdy leather sandals feature straps that wrap up to the knees. Etched in the leather are intricate patterns of stags leaping through the forest. You gain a +5-foot item bonus to your land Speed and a +3 item bonus on [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks when attempting to [[DATABASE/action/High Jump|High Jump]] or [[DATABASE/action/Long Jump|Long Jump]]. When you invest the sandals, you either increase your Strength score by 2 or increase it to 18, whichever is higher.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> envision **Frequency** once per minute; **Trigger** You attempt a High Jump or Long Jump but you didn't Stride at least 10 feet; **Effect** You can attempt the jump normally. It doesn't automatically fail.
