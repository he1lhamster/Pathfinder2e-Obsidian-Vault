# Clandestine Cloak<span class="item-type">Item 6+</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Illusion</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[Core Rulebook]] 
**Usage** worn cloak; **Bulk** L

---
When you pull up the hood of this nondescript gray cloak (an Interact action), you become drab and uninteresting, gaining a +1 item bonus to Stealth checks and to Deception checks to Impersonate a forgettable background character, such as a servant, but also taking a –1 item penalty to Diplomacy and Intimidation checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You pull the cloak’s hood up and gain the benefits of [[Nondetection]] for 1 hour or until you pull the hood back down, whichever comes first.
