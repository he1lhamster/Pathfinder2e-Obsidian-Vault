This snare is coated with [[DATABASE/equipment/Giant Wasp Venom|giant wasp venom]] and tipped with needles, which deliver the venom to the first creature to enter the square. That creature takes 4d6 damage (DC 23 basic Reflex) and, on a failure, the creature is exposed to the giant wasp venom.
**Craft Requirements** Supply 2 doses of giant wasp venom.
