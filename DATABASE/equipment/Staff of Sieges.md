This [[Adamantine Weapon]] [[Staff]] is fitted with mithral plates resembling battlements on a castle. Lesser cover, cover, and greater cover don't grant any bonus against your spell attacks or to saving throws against your spells that you cast from the staff. 
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
