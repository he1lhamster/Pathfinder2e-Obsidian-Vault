A _bound guardian_ is a [[DATABASE/monster/Tattoo Guardian|tattoo guardian]] bonded to your skin, which you can temporarily animate to protect you. If your soul leaves your body, your tattoo guardian might be destroyed.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">3</span> command, envision, Interact (conjuration) **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You animate your tattoo guardian, otherwise using the duration and other parameters of a 4th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Summon Construct|summon construct]] spell. You can have the guardian Fly Free as part of Activating your tattoo. If your tattoo guardian drops to 0 HP, the activation ends, and the inanimate tattoo returns to your skin.
