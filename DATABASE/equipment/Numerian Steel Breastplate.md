This _+1 [[DATABASE/equipment/Resilient|resilient]] [[DATABASE/armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ is constructed from the salvaged armor plating of one of Numeria's large robots. It protects the wearer from harsh elements and common laser fire, granting the wearer fire resistance 5.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You activate the remnants of mysterious technology within the breastplate to encase yourself in an electric field. For 1 minute, adjacent creatures that hit you with a melee Strike, as well as creatures that touch you or hit you with an unarmed attack, take 1d10 electricity damage per hit.
