This serum jolts the body to help shake off physical debilitations. When you drink this elixir, it attempts a counteract check with the listed counteract modifier to remove the [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy]] or [[DATABASE/condition/Enfeebled|enfeebled]] condition from a single source, using the source of that condition to determine the counteract level and DC.
