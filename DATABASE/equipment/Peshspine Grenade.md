Peshspine grenades are explosive bombs packed with alchemically treated needles from the pesh cactus. A peshspine grenade deals the listed piercing damage and splash damage. On a hit, the target gains the [[DATABASE/condition/Stupefied|stupefied]] condition until the end of its next turn.
 Many types of peshpine grenade grant an item bonus to attack rolls.

## Peshspine Grenade (Lesser)<span class="item-type">Item 1</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 149. Against the Scarlet Triad|Pathfinder #149: Against the Scarlet Triad]]
**Price** 3 gp
**Bulk** L
The bomb deals 1d6 piercing damage and 1 piercing splash damage, and the target is [[DATABASE/condition/Stupefied|stupefied 1]].
