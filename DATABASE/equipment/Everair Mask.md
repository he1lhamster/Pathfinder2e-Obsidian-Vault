This simple, mass-produced gaiter mask is standard issue for miners working in deep, dangerous mines. While wearing the mask, you gain a +1 item bonus to Fortitude saving throws against inhaled poisons. An _everair mask_ makes use of magical runes related to the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Air|Plane of Air]] to create breathable air.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You activate the mask's rune, and the air surrounding air, as well as the air you exhale, becomes enriched with oxygen. This allows the _everair mask_ to recycle air into breathable air for 1 hour. The rune's magic is unable to provide breathable air while underwater, in a vacuum, or in any other situation where air is normally unavailable.
