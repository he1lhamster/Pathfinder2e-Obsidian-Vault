A complex pattern of protective symbols gives protection against various forms of energy, but only part of the runic structure can be active at a given time.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> envision **Frequency** once per hour; **Trigger** You take acid, cold, electricity, or fire damage; **Effect** You gain resistance 5 to the triggering damage type. This doesn't apply to the triggering damage. This resistance lasts until you Activate this rune again or the armor is no longer invested by you.
