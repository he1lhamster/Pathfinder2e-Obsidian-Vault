# Stalk Goggles<span class="item-type">Item 1+</span>

<span class="trait-rare item-trait">Rare</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Morph</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 187. The Seventh Arch|Pathfinder #187: The Seventh Arch]]
**Usage** worn eyepiece; **Bulk** —

---
These black leather goggle frames have no lenses. Instead, when a character puts them on and invests them, the wearer's eyes transform and lengthen into snail-like eyestalks. The wobbly eyestalks stretch out through the lens holes and up over the wearer's head on lengthened optic nerves.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> envision **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** By focusing hard, you can watch for enemies in all directions. You gain [[DATABASE/monsterability/All-Around Vision|all-around vision]] for 1 minute; during this time, you can't be flanked.
