A silver charm bracelet, a _sluggish bracelet_ appears to be a [[DATABASE/equipment/Bracelet of Dashing|bracelet of dashing]], granting you a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks. If the curse goes unrecognized, you think you can Activate it to gain a +10-foot status bonus to your Speed for 1 minute. Instead, its activation is as follows.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> command ; **Effect** You take a –10-foot penalty to your Speed for 1 minute, and the bracelet fuses to you. Thereafter, it grants you no bonus to Acrobatics checks, and it imposes a –5-foot status penalty to your Speed.
