This portable silver anvil has a narrow hoop on one end, through which a long, sturdy chain has been strung. _[[DATABASE/deity/Torag|Torag's]] Silver Anvil_ can be wielded as a _+3 [[DATABASE/equipment/Holy|holy]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Flaming|greater flaming]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|greater striking]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Silver Weapon|silver]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Meteor Hammer|meteor hammer]]_. Outside of combat, the anvil can be used with a hammer as an incredibly effective portable forge, heating up the metal to be forged without a furnace and granting a +3 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Crafting|Crafting]] checks involving metalworking. When you use the anvil to successfully [[DATABASE/action/Repair|Repair]] a metal item, the item recovers an additional 10 Hit Points (or an additional 20 on a critical success).
