The _Deck of Harrowed Tales_ is a unique deck connected to a unique demiplane known as the Harrowed Realm.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** If on a plane other than the Harrowed Realm, the deck creates a [[DATABASE/spell/Gate|gate]] to the Harrowed Realm.
**Destruction** The _Deck of Harrowed Tales_ can be destroyed by completing a mythical series of 54 quests tied to each of the cards within. Only then can the final 55th quest be revealed, upon the completion of which the deck vanishes.
