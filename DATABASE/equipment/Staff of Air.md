Carved from white ash wood, a staff of air crackles with electrical sparks, and a breeze always follows the wielder. While wielding a _staff of air_, you feel lighter on your feet, and you can Step into difficult terrain once per round. 
**Activate** Cast a Spell ; **Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed levels of all listed spells.
