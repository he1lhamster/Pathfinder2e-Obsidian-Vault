**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> Command **Frequency** once per hour; **Trigger** You succeed at an attack roll to Strike with a weapon with the _hauling_ rune; **Effect** The target must succeed at a DC 20 Reflex save or be moved 5 feet in a direction you choose. This is forced movement.
