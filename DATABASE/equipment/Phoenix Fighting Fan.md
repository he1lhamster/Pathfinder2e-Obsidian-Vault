This elegant _+3 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|greater striking]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Flaming|flaming]] [[DATABASE/equipment/Silver Weapon|silver]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Fighting Fan|fighting fan]]_ features sharp silver feathers instead of traditional paper leaves in its design.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">3</span> command, envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You call upon the fan's extradimensional powers to trap a creature in Hao Jin's repository, a small demiplane that holds the countless relics Hao Jin collected over her lifetime (though a trapped creature cannot interact with any of these relics). The fighting fan casts [[DATABASE/spell/Maze|maze]] on a creature within 30 feet. The fan automatically Sustains the Spell as long as you Strike a creature with it each round. The [[DATABASE/skill/Survival|Survival]] or Perception DC to escape the demiplane is 34. The spell ends once the creature escapes, you fail to Strike a creature with the fan during a round, or after 1 minute, whichever comes first.
**Activate** 10 minutes (envision, Interact) ; **Effect** The fan casts 8th-level [[DATABASE/spell/Raise Dead|raise dead]], consuming the _phoenix fighting fan_ in the process.
