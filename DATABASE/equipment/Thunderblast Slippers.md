Unassuming in appearance, these slippers indicate their nature only with a signature strip of yellow stitching. You gain a +2 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You move like the wind, with precision and speed. You Stride up to 60 feet; this movement doesn't trigger reactions. When you stop, if you've moved at least 30 feet from where you started, you release a thunderous 5-foot emanation that deals 2d6 bludgeoning damage and 2d6 sonic damage with a DC 25 basic Fortitude save. A creature that critically fails its save is also knocked [[DATABASE/condition/Prone|prone]].
