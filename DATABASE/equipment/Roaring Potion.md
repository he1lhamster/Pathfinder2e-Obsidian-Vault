Ripples move constantly through a _roaring potion_, a cloudy liquid that growls when you open its container. Drinking it gives you access to two other activations for 1 hour.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> envision ; **Effect** You gain the effects of a [[DATABASE/spell/Bullhorn|bullhorn]] spell. You can Dismiss the activation.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> envision **Frequency** once every 1d4 rounds; **Effect** You emit a scream in a 15-foot cone that deals 4d4 sonic damage. Each creature in the area can attempt a DC 24 Fortitude saving throw.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage.
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and is [[DATABASE/condition/Frightened|frightened 1]].
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and is frightened 2.
