This suit of _+1 [[DATABASE/armor/Hide Armor|hide armor]]_ is sickly green and covered in thick warts and nodules, fashioned from the hide of a [[DATABASE/monster/Trollhound|trollhound]] and imbued with some of the beast's vitality. Wearing this armor gives you a –1 circumstance penalty to all checks made using [[DATABASE/skill/Diplomacy|Diplomacy]] to interact with [[DATABASE/monster/Troll|trolls]] and a +1 circumstance bonus to Diplomacy checks used to [[DATABASE/action/Make an Impression|Make an Impression]] in communities traditionally plagued by troll attacks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> Interact **Trigger** You take damage from a melee attack while you have half or fewer of your normal maximum Hit Points; **Effect** Your body knits itself back together, healing you for 3d8 Hit Points.
**Craft Requirements** The initial raw materials must include the hides of at least two trollhounds.
