# Triton's Conch<span class="item-type">Item 9</span>

<span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Price** 640 gp
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L

---
If you put this large opalescent conch shell to your ear, you can hear the sound of the roaring sea crashing against the shore.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact (auditory) ; **Effect** You can raise the conch to your lips and blow into it, letting out a long, rumbling note. For the next minute, you and all allies who were within 30 feet of you when you activated the conch gain a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks to Swim and can breathe under water.
