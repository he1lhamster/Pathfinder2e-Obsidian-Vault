While looking straight down the barrel of this _+1 [[DATABASE/equipment/Striking|striking]] [[DATABASE/weapon/Blunderbuss|blunderbuss]]_, the spark gun's magical core is visible amid several reflectors. All damage dealt by a _thundercrasher_ is sonic damage. On a critical hit, the target must succeed at a Fortitude save against your class DC or be [[DATABASE/condition/Deafened|deafened]] for 1 minute.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact **Frequency** once per hour; **Effect** You overload the _thundercrasher_ to emit chaotic sonic frequencies that soften earth and stone. When you next fire the _thundercrasher_ it also partially liquefies any natural earth or stone surfaces within range of its scatter trait, making the area difficult terrain
