# Pacifying<span class="item-type">Item 5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Enchantment</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[Treasure Vault]] , [[Pathfinder 157. Devil at the Dreaming Palace]]
**Price** 150 gp
**Usage** etched onto a weapon; **Bulk** —

---
This rune turns weapons into instruments of peacemaking.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> command (mental) **Trigger** You damage a creature with the etched weapon; **Effect** The damaged creature must succeed at a DC 20 Will save or be pacified. A pacified creature takes a –2 penalty to attack rolls on any attacks that aren't nonlethal for 1 minute, and the creature also experiences a clear psychic warning that they should stop making attacks that could kill.
