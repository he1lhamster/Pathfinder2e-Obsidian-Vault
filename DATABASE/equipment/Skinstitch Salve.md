# Skinstitch Salve<span class="item-type">Item 7</span>

<span class="item-trait">Alchemical</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Healing</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Advanced Player's Guide|Advanced Player's Guide]] 
**Price** 55 gp
**Usage** held in 1 hand; **Bulk** L

---
This sticky salve stubbornly holds wounds closed and encourages swift natural healing. You can activate the salve in either of the following ways.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact ; **Effect** You [[DATABASE/action/Administer First Aid|Administer First Aid]] without requiring [[DATABASE/equipment/Healer's Tools|healer's tools]]. You either gain a +2 item bonus to the [[DATABASE/skill/Medicine|Medicine]] check, or you can use the skinstitch salve's Medicine modifier of +13 instead of your own.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">4</span> Interact **Trigger** You [[DATABASE/action/Treat Wounds|Treat Wounds]] or use [[DATABASE/feat/Battle Medicine|Battle Medicine]]; **Effect** You gain a +2 item bonus to the triggering [[DATABASE/skill/Medicine|Medicine]] check. If you roll a success on the Medicine check, you get a critical success instead.
