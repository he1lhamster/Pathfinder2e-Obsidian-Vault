**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Core Rulebook|Core Rulebook]] 
**Usage** worn bracers; **Bulk** L

---
These bracers are made from plates of durable mithral and gleam like the summer sun.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">5</span> Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Trigger** A ranged weapon attack hits you but doesn’t critically hit; **Requirements** You are aware of the attack and not flat-footed; **Effect** The bracers send the missile off-course. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If this would cause the attack to be a failure, the attack misses you.
