This clay figurine resembles a white cat with a paw outstretched. When it’s activated, any allies within 10 feet who also just failed or critically failed a Reflex saving throw from the same source (such as a [[DATABASE/spell/Fireball|fireball]] spell) can also reroll their saving throw and use the better result.
