This silver signet ring changes to match the insignia of a lord or organization you serve (or your own face, if you serve no one else). It grants you a +2 item bonus to Diplomacy checks and lets you cast [[DATABASE/spell/Message|message]] as an arcane innate spell at will.
**Activate** 1 minute (envision) **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** The ring casts [[DATABASE/spell/Animal Messenger|animal messenger]] to your specification. The animal is a magical creature that springs from the ring, and its appearance suits the iconography or heraldry of the lord or organization represented by the ring.
