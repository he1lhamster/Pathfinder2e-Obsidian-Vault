A _helm of underwater action_ is a streamlined sallet with a visor and a flanged rear. While wearing the helmet with the visor down, you can see, hear, and speak clearly underwater. You also have a +1 item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] checks to [[DATABASE/action/Swim|Swim]].
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">3</span> command, envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You can breathe underwater for 8 hours. During this time, you have a swim Speed equal to your land Speed.
