# Privacy Ward Fulu<span class="item-type">Item 1+</span>

<span class="trait-rare item-trait">Rare</span><span class="item-trait">Abjuration</span><span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Fulu</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 196. The Summer That Never Was|Pathfinder #196: The Summer That Never Was]]
**Usage** affixed to wall; **Bulk** —

---
This fulu seeks to keep thieves, spies, and unwanted attention away from a room. A depiction of a lock appears in the center of this fulu, which is in turn surrounded by circles of broken keys. When applied to a wall inside a room, all creatures within the room gain an item bonus to [[DATABASE/skill/Stealth|Stealth]] checks against creatures outside the room.

## Privacy Ward Fulu (Hallway)<span class="item-type">Item 1</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 196. The Summer That Never Was|Pathfinder #196: The Summer That Never Was]]
**Price** 4 gp
The _privacy ward fulu_ can shield a room of up to 10 square feet, grants a +1 item bonus, and has a duration of 1 hour.

## Privacy Ward Fulu (Room)<span class="item-type">Item 5</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 196. The Summer That Never Was|Pathfinder #196: The Summer That Never Was]]
**Price** 25 gp
The fulu can shield a room of up to 30 square feet, grants a +1 item bonus, and has a duration of 8 hours. If you become [[DATABASE/condition/Hidden|hidden]] within the room, you automatically become [[DATABASE/condition/Undetected|undetected]] to creatures of the fulu's level or lower.

## Privacy Ward Fulu (Chamber)<span class="item-type">Item 9</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 196. The Summer That Never Was|Pathfinder #196: The Summer That Never Was]]
**Price** 120 gp
The fulu can shield a room of up to 90 square feet, grants a +2 item bonus, and has a duration of 24 hours. If you become hidden within the room, you automatically become undetected to creatures of the fulu's level or lower.
