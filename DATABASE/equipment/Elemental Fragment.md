# Elemental Fragment<span class="item-type">Item 10+</span>

**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> (concentrate, manipulate)

---
This chunk of solidified planar energy is as large as a walnut and comes in six different varieties: a clear piece of crystal for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Air|Plane of Air]], a rough piece of rock for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Earth|Plane of Earth]], a solidified piece of cooled lava for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Fire|Plane of Fire]], a compact piece of iron for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Metal|Plane of Metal]], a solid piece of ice for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Water|Plane of Water]], or a compact mass of plant matter for the [[DATABASE/plane/Plane of Wood|Plane of Wood]]. You crack the fragment as you activate it, unleashing the planar energy within. This energy casts a spell of your choice: a 5th-rank [[DATABASE/spell/Elemental Form|elemental form]] spell affecting you or a 5th-rank [[DATABASE/spell/Summon Elemental|summon elemental]] spell; if you summon an elemental, you can Sustain the activation to keep control of the elemental. The spell's element matches that of the fragment.
