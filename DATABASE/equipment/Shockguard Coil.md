This miniature Stasian coil talisman emits small sparks when jostled. It uses a combination of Stasian technology and evocation magic to erupt in electricity when discharged. When you activate the coil, the foe takes 2d12 electricity damage (DC 27 basic Reflex save). On a failed save, the foe is [[DATABASE/condition/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]] until the start of its next turn.
