**Usage** worn mask; **Bulk** —

These minor magic items are painted to resemble your face, or some metaphorical depiction thereof, to encourage the ushers of death to take the mask in your place. If you begin your turn with a [[DATABASE/condition/Dying|dying]] value of 3 or greater, instead of making your recovery check, you lose the dying condition but remain [[DATABASE/condition/Unconscious|unconscious]] at 0 Hit Points. The mask then cracks in half and is destroyed. The [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Psychopomp|psychopomps]] won't be so easily fooled again—you are temporarily immune to the effects of any _psychopomp mask_ for 1 year.
