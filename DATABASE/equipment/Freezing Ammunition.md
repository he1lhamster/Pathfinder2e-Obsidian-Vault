This chilly ammunition is dark blue and cold to the touch. When activated _freezing ammunition_ hits a target, the target must succeed at a DC 19 Fortitude save or be [[DATABASE/condition/Slowed|slowed 1]] for 1 round by the intense cold (slowed 1 for 1 minute on a critical failure).
