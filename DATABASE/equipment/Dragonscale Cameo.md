<span class="item-trait">Consumable</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span><span class="item-trait">Morph</span><span class="item-trait">Talisman</span><span class="item-trait">Transmutation</span>

**Price** 400 gp
**Usage** affixed to armor; **Bulk** —
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> envision

---
This ornamental pin, consisting of a single preserved dragon scale from a [[Dragon, Chromatic]] or [[Dragon, Metallic]] dragon mounted in a setting of precious metal, is typically affixed between the shoulder blades. When you Activate the pin, a pair of draconic wings matching the color of the scale unfurl from your shoulders, granting you a fly Speed of 50 feet for 5 minutes.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one scale from an adult or older dragon.
