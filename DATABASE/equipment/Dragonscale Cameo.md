This ornamental pin, consisting of a single preserved dragon scale from a [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Chromatic|chromatic]] or [[DATABASE/monsterfamily/Dragon, Metallic|metallic]] dragon mounted in a setting of precious metal, is typically affixed between the shoulder blades. When you Activate the pin, a pair of draconic wings matching the color of the scale unfurl from your shoulders, granting you a fly Speed of 50 feet for 5 minutes.
**Craft Requirements** Supply one scale from an adult or older dragon.
