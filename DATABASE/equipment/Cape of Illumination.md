This golden cape, embroidered with vibrant red sun rays, sparkles like sunlight reflecting off the ocean.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> command, Interact ; **Effect** The cape glows with the effects of 1st-level [[DATABASE/spell/Light|light]]. You can Activate the cape again to Dismiss the effect.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">2</span> envision, Interact **Frequency** once per day; **Effect** You draw the cape and unleash a blinding flash of light. All enemies within 30 feet that can see you must attempt a DC 19 Fortitude save.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Dazzled|dazzled]] for 1 round.
**Failure** The creature is [[DATABASE/condition/Blinded|blinded]] for 1 round and dazzled for 1 minute. The creature can spend an Interact action rubbing its eyes to end the blinded condition.
**Critical Failure** The creature is blinded for 1 round and dazzled for 1 hour.
