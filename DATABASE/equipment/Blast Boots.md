# Blast Boots<span class="item-type">Item 1+</span>

**Access** Due to the use of technology, all gadgets are uncommon or rare, though inventors and regions with access to inventors have access to uncommon gadgets.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">1</span> Interact

These sets of rockets come in pairs and strap onto existing footwear (or a creature's feet). Inserting them and aligning them properly takes 1 minute. When you Activate the blast boots, you can [[DATABASE/action/High Jump|High Jump]] or [[DATABASE/action/Long Jump|Long Jump]], without the need to Stride first. Higher-level versions increase the distance of your High Jump or Long Jump.

## Blast Boots (Lesser)<span class="item-type">Item 1</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 3 gp
**Bulk** L

## Blast Boots (Moderate)<span class="item-type">Item 3</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 11 gp
**Bulk** L
When you Activate the moderate blast boots to High Jump, you can increase the vertical distance of your High Jump by up to 30 feet. When you Activate them to Long Jump, you can increase the horizontal distance of your Long Jump by up to 45 feet.

## Blast Boots (Greater)<span class="item-type">Item 9</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 140 gp
**Bulk** L
When you Activate the greater blast boots to High Jump, you can increase the vertical distance of your High Jump by up to 50 feet. When you Activate them to Long Jump, you can increase the horizontal distance of your Long Jump by up to 75 feet. Additionally, for 1 minute, the greater blast boots continue to boost your jumps. Every time you [[DATABASE/action/Leap|Leap]] during the duration, you can move 30 feet in any direction, or your normal Leap distance, whichever is further.

## Blast Boots (Major)<span class="item-type">Item 17</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Guns & Gears|Guns & Gears]]
**Price** 2,750 gp
**Bulk** L
When you Activate the major blast boots to High Jump, you can increase the vertical distance of your High Jump by up to 80 feet. When you Activate them to Long Jump, you can increase the horizontal distance of your Long Jump by up to 120 feet. Additionally, for 1 minute, the major blast boots remain active, allowing you to clumsily fly through the air for the duration. You gain a 30-foot Fly speed for the duration, but any time you Fly, you are [[DATABASE/condition/Clumsy|clumsy 1]] until the start of your next turn.
