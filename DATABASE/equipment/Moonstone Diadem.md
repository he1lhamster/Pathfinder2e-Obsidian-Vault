# Moonstone Diadem<span class="item-type">Item 8</span>

<span class="trait-rare item-trait">Rare</span><span class="item-trait">Divination</span><span class="item-trait">Invested</span><span class="item-trait">Magical</span>
**Source** [[Pathfinder 152. Legacy of the Lost God]]
**Price** 500 gp
**Usage** worn circlet
; **Bulk** L

---
This delicate and elegant diadem is woven of intricate silver wires set with three tear-shaped pearlescent moonstones. You gain a +1 item bonus to [[Religion]] checks.
**Activate** <span class="action-icon">3</span> focus **Frequency** once per hour; **Requirements** You are within 10 feet of a _moonstone pool_; **Effect** You peer into the _moonstone pool_ and gain access to all the visual history recorded within the receptacle by concentrating on the specific subject you wish to see. Conversely, you can choose to deposit your own memories into the pool by concentrating for 1 hour on the information you wish to impart.
