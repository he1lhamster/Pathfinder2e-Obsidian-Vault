**Source** [[Legends]]
**Usage** varies by weapon; **Bulk** varies by weapon

---
Sovereign steel weapons are treated as [[Cold Iron Weapon]] against creatures with a weakness to cold iron, like [[Demon]] and fey. In addition, the [[Noqual]] in sovereign steel weapons disrupts spellcasters' concentration, causing them to become [[Stupefied]] for 1 round on a critical hit.
