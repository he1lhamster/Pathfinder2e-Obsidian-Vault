# Augdunar

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Highhelm|Highhelm]]
Your companion is an [[DATABASE/monster/Augdunar|augdunar]], a powerful breed of mule favored by the [[DATABASE/ancestry/Dwarf|dwarves]] of Highhelm.
**Size** Medium
**Melee** <span class="action-icon">1</span> hoof, **Damage** 1d8 bludgeoning
**Str** +3, **Dex** +1, **Con** +3, **Int** -4, **Wis** +1, **Cha** +0
**Hit Points** 10
**Skill** [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] 
**Senses** [[DATABASE/monsterability/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]], [[DATABASE/monsterability/Scent|scent]] (imprecise) 30 feet
**Speed** 25 feet

**Support Benefit** The augdunar shifts its weight, proffering you an item. You Interact to draw a weapon or item that is being carried by the augdunar.
**Advanced Maneuver** Weighted Kick

### [[DATABASE/action/Weighted Kick|Weighted Kick]] <span class="action-icon">2</span>

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Highhelm|Highhelm]]

---
Your augdunar gives a heavy kick with its hind legs, bringing the weight of its burden on the target. It makes a hoof Strike. On a successful hit, the augdunar automatically [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shoves]] the target back 5 feet (10 feet on a critical success).
