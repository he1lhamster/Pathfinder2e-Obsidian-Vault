**Str** +3, **Dex** +1, **Con** +2, **Int** -4, **Wis** +2, **Cha** +0

**Senses** low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet

**Support Benefit** Your boar gores your foes. Until the start of your next turn, your Strikes that damage a creature in your boar's reach also deal 1d6 [[Persistent Damage]]. If your boar is nimble or savage, the persistent bleed damage increases to 2d6.

### [[Boar Charge]] <span class="action-icon">2</span>

**Source** [[Advanced Player's Guide]]
