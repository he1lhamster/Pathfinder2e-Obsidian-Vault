# Staff Acrobat

**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 151. The Show Must Go On|Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On]]
You can perform amazing acts in and out of combat when you have a spear, staff, or polearm.

## [[DATABASE/feat/Staff Acrobat Dedication|Staff Acrobat Dedication]] <span class="item-type">Feat 2</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Dedication</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/Pathfinder 151. The Show Must Go On|Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On]]
**Archetype** [[DATABASE/archetype/Staff Acrobat|Staff Acrobat]]
**Prerequisites** Dexterity 16; trained in [[DATABASE/skill/Acrobatics|Acrobatics]] and [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]]; trained with at least one of the following weapons: [[DATABASE/weapon/Staff|staff]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Bo Staff|bo staff]], [[DATABASE/weapon/Halfling Sling Staff|halfling sling staff]], or any weapon in the [[DATABASE/weapongroup/Spear|spear]] or [[DATABASE/weapongroup/Polearm|polearm]] group (referred to in this archetype as “your staff”)

---
When you [[DATABASE/action/High Jump|High Jump]], [[DATABASE/action/Leap|Leap]], or [[DATABASE/action/Long Jump|Long Jump]] while wielding your staff, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to any [[DATABASE/skill/Athletics|Athletics]] check required and add 5 feet to the distance you can Leap vertically and horizontally. You can [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shove]] and [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]] even if you don’t have a free hand, provided you are wielding your staff. Finally, when you roll a success on a check to [[DATABASE/action/Balance|Balance]] while wielding your staff, you get a critical success instead.
**Special** You cannot select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the staff acrobat archetype.

---
You can attempt to [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shove]] or [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]] creatures up to two sizes larger than you. If you have master proficiency with your staff, you can attempt to Shove or Trip creatures up to three sizes larger than you.

**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Staff Acrobat Dedication|Staff Acrobat Dedication]]

---
You sweep your foes away with your staff. Roll an Athletics check to [[DATABASE/action/Shove|Shove]] or [[DATABASE/action/Trip|Trip]], and compare the result to the appropriate DCs of up to two foes, each of whom must be within your reach and no more than 5 feet apart.

## [[DATABASE/feat/Levering Strike|Levering Strike]] <span class="action-icon">2</span> <span class="item-type">Feat 8</span>

<span class="trait-uncommon item-trait">Uncommon</span><span class="item-trait">Archetype</span><span class="item-trait">Press</span>

**Prerequisites** [[DATABASE/feat/Staff Acrobat Dedication|Staff Acrobat Dedication]]
