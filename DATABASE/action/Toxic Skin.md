# Toxic Skin <span class="action-icon">5</span>

<span class="item-trait">Grippli</span><span class="item-trait">Poison</span>
**Source** [[DATABASE/source/The Mwangi Expanse|The Mwangi Expanse]] 
**Frequency** once per hour
**Trigger** A creature touches you, such as by [[DATABASE/action/Grapple|Grappling]] you, successfully hitting you with an unarmed attack, or using a touch-range spell against you.

---
You exude a deadly toxin. The triggering creature takes 1d4 poison damage (basic Fortitude save using your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher). At 3rd level and every 2 levels thereafter, the damage increases by 1d4.
